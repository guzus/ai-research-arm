## 2026-01-14 - Emojis as Visual Anchors in Text-Heavy Reports
**Learning:** In text-heavy automated reports (like Markdown digests), users struggle to scan for sections. Emojis serve as effective visual anchors that break up wall-of-text monotony without requiring complex HTML/CSS.
**Action:** When designing text-based outputs, use consistent emoji sets for headers to improve scanability and add a touch of delight.
