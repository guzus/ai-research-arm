- **Use boring languages with LLMs** (206pts, 149 comments): Thesis that LLMs perform dramatically better on well-documented, stable languages (Python, JS, SQL) than on newer or niche ones because training data volume matters more than language expressiveness. Practical advice for prompt engineering — [link](https://jry.io/writing/use-boring-languages-with-llms/)
