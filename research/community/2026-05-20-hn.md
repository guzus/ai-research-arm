- **Karpathy joins Anthropic**: By far the biggest AI story of the day — Andrej Karpathy announced he's joining Anthropic. 1,158 points and nearly 500 comments, making it the top post on HN. Enormous signal for Anthropic's momentum and talent acquisition. - https://twitter.com/karpathy/status/2056753169888334312

Community actively comparing it to Claude Haiku and GPT-4o-mini tiers. - https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-5/

- **Gemini Omni**: A second Google model release alongside Flash — Gemini Omni targets full multimodal (audio, vision, text, code) real-time use cases. 257 points, 106 comments. - https://deepmind.google/models/gemini-omni/

- **Show HN: Forge (8B → 99% agentic accuracy)**: An open-source guardrail framework that improves an 8B model's agentic task success from 53% to 99%. - **OpenAI + Google SynthID**: OpenAI adopts Google's SynthID watermarking standard for AI-generated images, plus launches a verification tool. 181 points, 96 comments. Marks a rare cross-company standardization effort on provenance. - https://openai.com/index/advancing-content-provenance/

- **Mistral acquires Emmi AI**: Mistral makes its first major acquisition, picking up Emmi AI (enterprise AI stack). 159 points, 38 comments. Seen as Mistral's push to compete with vertically integrated offerings from OpenAI and Anthropic. - https://www.emmi.ai/news/mistral-ai-acquires-emmi-ai

- **Remove-AI-Watermarks tool**: Open-source CLI for stripping AI watermarks from images. 99 points and active debate on the ethics and legality of watermark removal vs. creative freedom. - https://github.com/wiltodelta/remove-ai-watermarks

- **Gemini CLI deprecation**: Google announced Gemini CLI will be replaced by Antigravity CLI on June 18, 2026. Small story (41 pts) but relevant for developers with Gemini integrations. - https://developers.googleblog.com/an-important-update-transitioning-gemini-cli-to-antigravity-cli/
