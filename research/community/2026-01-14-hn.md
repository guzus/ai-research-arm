#### Security & Trust

- **[Notion AI: Unpatched data exfiltration](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46531565)**: Critical vulnerability allows data exfiltration via indirect prompt injection. AI document edits are saved before user approval, enabling malicious image insertion that exfiltrates sensitive data (salary expectations, candidate feedback, internal role details, diversity hiring goals) through URL parameters. Timeline: reported 12/24/2025, closed as non-applicable 12/29/2025, public disclosure 01/07/2026. Discussions focus on treating LLM outputs as untrusted and implementing cybersecurity guardrails. - [Source](https://www.promptarmor.com/resources/notion-ai-unpatched-data-exfiltration)

- **Reflections on AI at the End of 2025**: Concerns about public trust in LLM outputs and hallucinations in everyday usage. Software engineers can verify AI-generated code, but general public usage in medical and life advice raises red flags.
