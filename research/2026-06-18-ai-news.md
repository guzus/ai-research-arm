### Net-New — Midjourney Medical: AI Image Lab Bets Its Next Act on a Full-Body Ultrasound Machine

**Date:** June 18, 2026 (event teased June 17, held 6pm PT SF; not in prior reports) | **Sources:** [Midjourney — A New Era of Midjourney (Medical)](https://www.midjourney.com/medical/blogpost), [Bloomberg — AI Startup Midjourney Pivots to Health With Ultrasound Machine](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-18/ai-startup-midjourney-pivots-to-health-with-ultrasound-machine), [Engadget — Midjourney is developing a full-body ultrasonic scanner](https://www.engadget.com/2196998/midjourney-full-body-ultrasonic-scanner/), [Let's Data Science — Midjourney Launches Full-Body Ultrasound Scanner, Plans Spa](https://letsdatascience.com/news/midjourney-launches-full-body-ultrasound-scanner-plans-spa-706a2786), [Startup Fortune — Midjourney bets its next act on a full-body ultrasonic scanner and a spa chain](https://startupfortune.com/midjourney-is-betting-its-next-act-on-a-full-body-ultrasonic-scanner-and-a-spa-chain/)

- **What's net-new:** CEO **David Holz** unveiled the **Midjourney Scanner** — a **full-body ultrasonic CT** device using **no radiation and no magnets** ("just sound, water, and a ~60-second scan"). The scanner is built from a ring of roughly **half a million tiny squares** (each ~the size of a grain of sand), every square acting as both a micro-speaker and micro-microphone — emitting ultrasonic waves and recording the returning ripples millions of times per second to reconstruct a 3D body image. Coverage also cites a transducer count on the order of **~8,960** active elements per the SF demo writeups.
- **The "Spa" go-to-market:** Rather than selling to hospitals, Midjourney plans a consumer venue — the **first Midjourney Spa opens in San Francisco (Union Square) in 2027**, pairing hot tubs/saunas/cold plunges with scan rooms. Stated economics: **a few dollars per session**.
- **Scale ambition:** Midjourney says it aims to deploy **~50,000 scanners worldwide over ~6 years** and reach **a billion full-body scans per month by ~2031**.
- **Why it matters:** A pure-software generative-AI lab (best known for text-to-image) making an unannounced **hardware + consumer-health** pivot is a notable strategic break — and the only net-new dated *product* announcement in the June 17→18 window, alongside the UNIDIR Geneva conference (above). Treat the medical/diagnostic claims as company-stated; no peer-reviewed validation or regulatory clearance (e.g., FDA) was disclosed at launch.
