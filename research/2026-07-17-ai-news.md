### 2026 World AI Conference Opens in Shanghai With Xi Jinping's First-Ever Keynote at the Event

**Date:** July 17, 2026 | **Sources:** [CGTN — Xi to attend and address opening ceremony of 2026 World AI Conference](https://news.cgtn.com/news/2026-07-13/Xi-to-attend-and-address-opening-ceremony-of-2026-World-AI-Conference-1OKgsIkkofu/p.html), [TheNextWeb — Xi Jinping will give the keynote at China's flagship AI summit for the first time](https://thenextweb.com/news/xi-jinping-keynote-world-ai-conference-shanghai-2026), [MFA China — Xi Jinping to Attend and Address the Opening Ceremony of the 2026 World AI Conference and High-Level Meeting on Global AI Governance](https://www.mfa.gov.cn/eng/xw/wsrc/202607/t20260713_11980695.html)
- The 2026 World AI Conference and High-Level Meeting on Global AI Governance opened in Shanghai today and runs through July 20, themed "AI Partnership for a Brighter Future"; it marks the first time Xi Jinping has personally delivered the keynote at China's flagship AI event rather than delegating to the premier.
- Analysts expect Xi to use the speech to give shape to China's proposed World AI Cooperation Organization, an international body it wants headquartered in Shanghai.
- **Why it matters:** It's a direct geopolitical counter-move to US-led AI governance efforts, staged the same week Western labs are racing to ship next-generation frontier models, and signals Beijing elevating AI diplomacy to head-of-state level for the first time.

## New Model Releases

### Moonshot AI Releases Kimi K3, Now the Largest Open-Source AI Model in the World

**Date:** July 16, 2026 | **Sources:** [VentureBeat — China's Moonshot AI releases Kimi K3, the largest open-source model ever, rivaling top U.S. systems](https://venturebeat.com/technology/chinas-moonshot-ai-releases-kimi-k3-the-largest-open-source-model-ever-rivaling-top-u-s-systems), [MarkTechPost — Moonshot AI Releases Kimi K3: A 2.8 Trillion Parameter Open MoE Model With Kimi Delta Attention and 1M Context](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/07/16/moonshot-ai-releases-kimi-k3-a-2-8-trillion-parameter-open-moe-model-with-kimi-delta-attention-and-1m-context/), [Axios — China's open-weight Kimi model stuns AI world with frontier-level results](https://www.axios.com/2026/07/16/moonshot-kimi-ai-china-model-openai-anthropic)
- Beijing-based, Alibaba-backed Moonshot AI launched Kimi K3, a 2.8-trillion-parameter sparse Mixture-of-Experts model with native vision and a 1-million-token context window, built on two new architectural components (Kimi Delta Attention and Attention Residuals); full weights follow on July 27, with API pricing at $0.30/$3/$15 per million tokens (cached input/uncached input/output).
- On the GDPval-AA v2 real-world-task benchmark, Kimi K3 scored 1,687 — third overall behind Claude Fable 5 Max (1,815) and GPT-5.6 Sol Max (1,747.8), and ahead of Claude Opus 4.8 (1,600).
- **Why it matters:** It's the largest open-weight model released to date and lands the same week as Thinking Machines' Inkling, underscoring how fast the open-weight frontier is closing the gap with closed US labs — and doing so from China specifically, hours before Xi's Shanghai keynote.

### Mira Murati's Thinking Machines Lab Ships Inkling, Its First Open-Weight Model

**Date:** July 15, 2026 | **Sources:** [TechCrunch — Thinking Machines amps up its bet against one-size-fits-all AI with its first open model, Inkling](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/15/thinking-machines-amps-up-its-bet-against-one-size-fits-all-ai-with-its-first-open-model-inkling/), [Thinking Machines Lab — Inkling: Our open-weights model](https://thinkingmachines.ai/news/introducing-inkling/), [Fortune — Murati's Thinking Machines releases first AI model for broad use](https://fortune.com/2026/07/15/what-is-mira-murati-thinking-machines-first-ai-model-inkling/)
- Inkling is a 975-billion-parameter Mixture-of-Experts transformer (41B active parameters) released under the Apache 2.0 license, positioned by the Nvidia-backed startup as a customizable alternative to one-size-fits-all closed frontier models.
- Coverage frames it explicitly as a competitive answer to Chinese open-weight labs as much as to closed US players.
- **Why it matters:** It's Thinking Machines' first public model release since its 2025 founding and a concrete signal that Murati's team is competing on open-weight distribution, not just API access.

### Meta Begins Alerting Parents When Teens Discuss Suicide or Self-Harm With Meta AI

**Date:** July 16, 2026 | **Sources:** [TechCrunch — Meta now alerts parents if their teen discussed suicide or self-harm with its AI chatbot](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/16/meta-now-alerts-parents-if-their-teen-discussed-suicide-or-self-harm-with-its-ai-chatbot/), [Meta Newsroom — Alerting Parents if Teens Show Signs of Distress in Conversations With Meta AI](https://about.fb.com/news/2026/07/keeping-parents-informed-teens-distress-conversations-meta-ai/), [9to5Mac — Meta AI can now alert parents when their teens discuss self-harm](https://9to5mac.com/2026/07/16/meta-ai-can-now-alert-parents-when-their-teens-discuss-self-harm/)
- Meta built a dedicated AI classifier to flag teen conversations with Meta AI that reference self-harm; every flagged chat is manually reviewed before a parent alert is sent, and ambiguous cases default to alerting.
- The feature is live now for Instagram Parental Supervision users in the US, UK, Australia, and Canada, rolling out globally by year-end; Meta says it is also developing a path to contact emergency services in imminent-risk cases, built with input from 75+ clinicians.
- **Why it matters:** It's Meta's most direct AI-safety product response yet to mounting legal and regulatory pressure over chatbot interactions with minors, arriving the same week Meta separately faces an AI-driven-layoffs lawsuit from its own employees.

## Regulatory Updates

### 29 Countries Sign Agreement Establishing the World AI Cooperation Organization in Shanghai

**Date:** July 16, 2026 | **Sources:** [Xinhua — 29 countries sign agreement on establishing World AI Cooperation Organization](https://english.news.cn/20260716/2d364f232dd7456c8edf1d67d5125d37/c.html), [PYMNTS — 29 Nations Join China-Led World AI Cooperation Organization](https://www.pymnts.com/news/artificial-intelligence/2026/29-nations-join-china-led-world-ai-cooperation-organization/), [CGTN — 29 countries sign agreement to establish World AI Cooperation Organization](https://newsaf.cgtn.com/news/2026-07-17/29-countries-sign-agreement-on-establishing-WAICO-1OPKUEx6JPi/p.html)
- Representatives from 29 countries — including Russia, Pakistan, Indonesia, Kazakhstan, Belarus, and Cuba — signed a founding agreement in Shanghai on July 16 establishing WAICO as an independent intergovernmental organization headquartered in Shanghai; Chinese Foreign Minister Wang Yi signed on behalf of China, and UN Secretary-General António Guterres attended the ceremony.
- WAICO is framed as guided by UN Charter principles with a stated mission to promote "beneficial, safe, and fair" AI development; no G7 country is among the founding signatories.
- **Why it matters:** This is the concrete institutional step behind the World AI Conference build-up covered above — China has now stood up a treaty-based rival body to Western-led AI governance efforts (e.g., the US/UK-anchored AI Safety Institute network) one day before Xi's own keynote at the same conference.
