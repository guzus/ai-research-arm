## Breaking News

### SpaceX Closes Up 19% on Nasdaq Debut — Largest IPO Ever Completes Above $2T

**Date:** June 12, 2026 | **Sources:** [NPR — SpaceX IPO makes history as largest ever; stock gains 19% on first day](https://www.npr.org/2026/06/12/nx-s1-5855004/stock-ai-spacex-ipo-elon-musk), [CNBC — SpaceX stock jumps 19%, closing near $161 after record IPO: Live updates](https://www.cnbc.com/amp/2026/06/12/spacex-ipo-spcx-live-updates.html), [Investing.com — SpaceX (SPCX)](https://www.investing.com/equities/spacex)

- **What happened:** SpaceX (Nasdaq: **SPCX**) made its trading debut Friday and **closed up ~19% at $160.95**, against the **$135** IPO offer price.
- **Scale:** The company **raised ~$75 billion** selling **more than 555 million shares** at $135 — the **largest IPO in recorded history** (surpassing Saudi Aramco's 2019 $29.4B). The first-day pop pushed the entity's market value **above $2 trillion**, making it one of the world's biggest listed companies on its first day.
- **Why it's AI news:** SpaceX is the combined home of Elon Musk's frontier-lab-adjacent stack — the **Colossus** supercomputer, the **Grok** model family, and the federal Grok contract. The public listing means the market now has a **daily price read** on an xAI-linked asset for the first time. It also resets the comp set for the pending **OpenAI** (>$1T target, Sept-2026-earliest window) and **Anthropic** ($965B, ~Oct target) listings — a successful mega-debut de-risks those windows.
- **Net-new vs. prior report:** The June 12 report covered the *pricing mechanics* and flagged the debut as today's *live* event. **This is the resolved outcome** — the +19% close and the >$2T first-day valuation, which were not knowable when the prior report was written.
