## Breaking News

### Bezos's Prometheus Raises $12B at ~$41B — Industrial AI's First Megaround

**Date:** June 11, 2026 | **Sources:** [Bloomberg — Jeff Bezos' AI Startup Valued at $41 Billion in Funding Round](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-11/jeff-bezos-ai-startup-valued-at-41-billion-in-funding-round), [Axios — Prometheus, the industrial AI startup from Jeff Bezos, is now worth $41 billion](https://www.axios.com/2026/06/11/prometheus-bezos-industrial-ai), [GeekWire — Bezos' AI startup Prometheus raises $12B at $41B valuation, and the CEOs explain what they're doing](https://www.geekwire.com/2026/bezos-ai-startup-prometheus-raises-12b-at-41b-valuation-and-the-ceos-explain-what-theyre-doing/), [CNBC — Bezos opens up about AI startup Prometheus after $12 billion raise: 'We're not being secretive'](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/11/project-prometheus-bezos-bajaj-live-updates.html), [PYMNTS — Jeff Bezos Raises $12 Billion for AI Engineering Startup Prometheus](https://www.pymnts.com/news/investment-tracker/2026/jeff-bezos-raises-12-billion-for-ai-engineering-startup-prometheus/)

- **What was announced:** Prometheus — the AI startup where Jeff Bezos is co-CEO alongside former Google/Verily executive **Vik Bajaj** — disclosed Thursday morning that it raised **$12 billion in Series B funding at a valuation of roughly $41 billion.**
- **Who's in:** Investors include **JPMorgan, BlackRock, Goldman Sachs, DST Global and Arch Venture Partners.** Bezos — who was the largest backer of the company's earlier **~$6.2B Series A** — participated in the new round as well.
- **What it does:** Prometheus (launched November 2025) is building AI to help engineers **design and manufacture physical products** — its stated ambition is an "**artificial general engineer**," a system meant to take a complex physical product (e.g., a jet engine, a medical device, consumer electronics) from concept through production. It frames AI's next frontier as the *physical* world, not just software/chat.
- **Scale of the bet:** The company has **~150 employees** and offices in **San Francisco, London and Zurich.** A $41B valuation for a roughly one-year-old, pre-product industrial-AI firm is one of the largest early-stage marks in the current cycle.
- **Why it matters:** This is the most concrete signal yet that the AI capital wave is broadening beyond chatbots and coding agents into **AI-for-manufacturing / "industrial AI."** It also further cements Bezos as a frontline AI principal (not just an investor), and adds a third Bezos-linked AI bet to the week's headlines after the smaller **Flourish** round (do not conflate the two).
- **Disambiguation:** Prometheus ≠ Flourish. Flourish (~$500M, Bezos/Lux/GV — covered June 10–11) is a separate company. Prometheus is the $12B/$41B industrial-engineering startup.
