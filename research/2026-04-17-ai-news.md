### Anthropic Launches Claude Design — Research Preview for Visual Creation
**Date:** April 17, 2026 | **Sources:** [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-design-anthropic-labs), [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/17/anthropic-launches-claude-design-a-new-product-for-creating-quick-visuals/), [Canva Newsroom](https://www.canva.com/newsroom/news/canva-claude-design/)

Anthropic Labs unveiled **Claude Design**, a new research-preview product that generates websites, landing pages, presentations, prototypes, and marketing collateral from natural-language prompts. It is powered by the just-released Claude Opus 4.7.

- Outputs: interactive prototypes, pitch decks (exportable as PPTX or to Canva), wireframes, landing pages, social assets, and code-powered "frontier design" work with voice, video, shaders, and 3D.
- Auto-builds a per-org design system by reading the customer's codebase and design files during onboarding, then applies the colors, typography, and components across projects.
- Inputs: text prompts, images, DOCX/PPTX/XLSX, full codebases, and web capture. Exports: internal URLs, folders, Canva, PDF, PPTX, or standalone HTML.
- Availability: research preview for Claude Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise subscribers, with gradual rollout through April 17. Canva's Visual Suite is an integration partner.
- Positions Anthropic directly into the Canva / Figma / Framer adjacent market and extends the Claude Labs surface beyond Claude Code.

### OpenAI Debuts GPT-Rosalind — Specialized Model for Drug Discovery
**Date:** April 17, 2026 | **Sources:** [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-rosalind/), [LA Times](https://www.latimes.com/business/story/2026-04-17/openai-takes-on-google-with-new-ai-designed-to-speed-drug-discovery), [FierceBiotech](https://www.fiercebiotech.com/biotech/openai-launches-biotech-specific-ai-model-gpt-rosalind), [The Next Web](https://thenextweb.com/news/openai-gpt-rosalind-life-sciences-drug-discovery-ai-model), [Euronews](https://www.euronews.com/health/2026/04/17/what-to-know-about-openais-new-model-for-life-sciences-research-gpt-rosalind)

OpenAI released **GPT-Rosalind**, a reasoning model purpose-built for biochemistry, genomics, and protein engineering — named for Rosalind Franklin.

- Use cases: evidence synthesis, hypothesis generation, experimental planning, multi-step scientific workflows. Compresses the 10–15 year target-to-approval timeline.
- Tooling: can query specialized databases, parse scientific literature, call computational tools, and suggest experimental pathways in one interface. A companion **Life Sciences research plugin for Codex** connects to 50+ scientific tools and data sources.
- Benchmarks: **0.751 pass rate on BixBench** (real-world comp-bio tasks); outperformed GPT-5.4 on 6 of 11 LABBench2 tasks, with particular strength in molecular cloning protocols.
- Launch partners: **Amgen, Moderna, Thermo Fisher Scientific, Allen Institute**, plus collaboration with **Los Alamos National Laboratory** on AI-guided protein and catalyst design.
- Access gating: U.S.-only trusted-access program; OpenAI restricted general availability over biosecurity concerns (pathogen-design misuse). Available as a research preview in ChatGPT, Codex, and the API.

### TechCrunch/Adobe: AI Traffic to U.S. Retailers Up 393% YoY in Q1 2026
**Date:** April 16, 2026 | **Sources:** [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/16/ai-traffic-to-us-retailers-rose-393-in-q1-and-its-boosting-their-revenue-too/), [Business20Channel](https://business20channel.tv/us-retailers-see-393-ai-traffic-surge-in-q1-2026-16-april-2026)

Adobe Analytics data reported April 16 shows AI-assistant-driven shopping traffic to U.S. retail sites surged **393% year-over-year in Q1 2026**.

- March 2026: **+269%** AI traffic vs. prior 12 months, building on **693%** growth in late-2025 holidays.
- AI visitors converted **+42%**, spent **+48%** more time on-site, browsed **+13%** more pages, and generated **+37%** higher revenue per visit vs. non-AI traffic — a full reversal from a year earlier.
- 39% of surveyed U.S. shoppers used AI for shopping; 66% said they trusted AI results for accuracy.
- ~25% of retailer homepages/category pages and **34% of product pages** remain unoptimized for LLMs, a visible friction point.
- Projection: AI traffic could hit **25–30% of total e-commerce by Q4 2026**, up from 8–12% today.

## New Model Releases

### Claude Opus 4.7 (April 16) — Full Details
**Date:** April 16, 2026 | **Sources:** [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-opus-4-7), [Claude Platform Docs](https://platform.claude.com/docs/en/about-claude/models/whats-new-claude-4-7), [AWS Blog](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/aws/introducing-anthropics-claude-opus-4-7-model-in-amazon-bedrock/), [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/04/16/anthropic-claude-opus-model-mythos)

Anthropic shipped **Claude Opus 4.7** on April 16, the largest step in the 4.x line and the model that powers today's Claude Design launch.

- **Coding / agents:** material gains on long-running software engineering tasks; verifies its own outputs before returning; produces cleaner code (fewer wrapper functions and fallback scaffolding).
- **Vision:** higher-resolution image support; improved pointing, measuring, counting, and bounding-box localization on natural images.
- **Instruction following:** more literal, especially at lower effort levels — no longer silently generalizes instructions. Response length calibrates to task complexity.
- **New `xhigh` effort tier** between `high` and `max`. Claude Code defaults to `xhigh` for all plans. Recommended starting tier for coding/agentic use cases: `high` or `xhigh`. New **"task budgets"** public beta to guide token spend across long runs.
- **Context:** 1M-token window, 128k max output. Updated tokenizer requires ~1.0–1.35× more tokens on the same input depending on content type.
- **Safety:** built-in detection and blocking for prohibited / high-risk cyber use; new **Cyber Verification Program** for vetted security professionals.
- **Pricing (unchanged):** $5 / 1M input, $25 / 1M output tokens. Available in all Claude products, API, Bedrock, Vertex AI, and Microsoft Foundry.
- Axios notes Anthropic acknowledges Opus 4.7 still does not match the capabilities of its unreleased **Claude Mythos Preview** (restricted access).
