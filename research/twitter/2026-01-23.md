# AI News - January 23, 2026

## 08:00 UTC

### Breaking News

- **OpenAI Releases GPT-5.2** - OpenAI announced today the rollout of GPT-5.2 (Instant, Thinking, and Pro versions) starting with paid plans. GPT-5.2 Thinking is their first model that performs at or above human expert level on GDPval, beating or tying top industry professionals on 70.9% of comparisons. Both Pro and Thinking now support a new "xhigh" reasoning effort level. Priced at $1.75/1M input tokens and $14/1M output tokens. - [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-2/)

- **Anthropic Publishes 23,000-Word "Constitution" for Claude** - On January 22, Anthropic released a revised Claude Constitution that addresses Claude directly and outlines its intended values and identity. The document acknowledges uncertainty about Claude's moral status and embeds hard constraints forbidding assistance with bioweapons, cyberweapons, CSAM, and illegitimate power seizures. This represents a significant expansion from the 2023 version which was just ~2,700 words. - [The Register](https://www.theregister.com/2026/01/22/anthropic_claude_constitution/)

- **Google Announces Gemini Integration with Classroom** - At BETT 2026 (January 22), Google announced deeper integration between Gemini and Google Classroom, with Gemini able to collaborate directly with Classroom using real class context. Google also expanded collaboration with Khan Academy, with Gemini models powering Khan Academy's Writing Coach. - [ETIH EdTech News](https://www.edtechinnovationhub.com/news/bett-2026-google-announces-major-ai-updates-to-gemini-and-google-classroom)

- **NVIDIA Physical AI Models at CES 2026** - NVIDIA released new physical AI models including Isaac GR00T N1.6 (an open reasoning VLA model for humanoid robots) and Cosmos Reason 2 (a leaderboard-topping reasoning VLM). Also released Nemotron Speech with leaderboard-topping open models for real-time, low-latency speech recognition. - [NVIDIA](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/nvidia-releases-new-physical-ai-models-as-global-partners-unveil-next-generation-robots)

### Industry Trends

- **Anthropic Fundraising** - Anthropic is reportedly seeking $10 billion in funding to raise the company's valuation to $350 billion, which would solidify its position as one of the most valuable AI startups. - [Silicon Republic](https://www.siliconrepublic.com/business/claude-maker-anthropic-to-raise-10bn-round-at-350bn-valuation-reports-ai)

- **Agentic AI Becomes Standard** - Anthropic's Model Context Protocol (MCP) has been donated to the Linux Foundation's new Agentic AI Foundation. OpenAI and Microsoft have publicly embraced MCP, which is quickly becoming the standard for agentic AI. - [Mean CEO Blog](https://blog.mean.ceo/ai-news-january-2026/)

- **Yann LeCun's World Model Lab** - Yann LeCun has left Meta to start his own world model lab, reportedly seeking a $5 billion valuation, signaling that 2026 will be a big year for world models. - [Understanding AI](https://www.understandingai.org/p/17-predictions-for-ai-in-2026)
