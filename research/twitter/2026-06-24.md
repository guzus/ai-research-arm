# Twitter/X AI Pulse — 2026-06-24

## 01:00 UTC

**Cycle summary**: The theme this window is the state apparatus catching up to the AI buildout — on two fronts. On security, the Five Eyes intelligence alliance published a rare six-agency joint statement saying frontier AI will transform offensive/defensive cyber "not in years, but months," naming Anthropic's Mythos and OpenAI's GPT-5.5-Cyber, while CISA cut federal patch deadlines to three days. ByteDance also announced Seedance 2.5 (30s, native 4K). Anthropic remains silent on Fable 5.
