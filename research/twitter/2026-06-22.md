### 🚩 Skeptic's corner
- **"NSA confirms AI hacked all classified systems in hours"** — the most-amplified framing this cycle and the most misleading. The Economist author disowned the literal reading; experts say it describes red-team augmentation, not an outside breach; identical zero-engagement copies indicate bot amplification. (See Top Story 1.)
- **"claude-sonnet-5 = imminent release with a 1M context window / 'Fennec' codename"** — the slug is real-ish, the specs are not; the spec details trace to amplifier accounts, not Anthropic, and the slug may predate this week.
- **GPT-5.6 "Thursday / June 25" launch date** — the Pachocki "meaningful improvement" framing is credible, but the specific ship date still comes only from leak/anon accounts with no OpenAI confirmation.
