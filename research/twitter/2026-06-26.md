### 🚩 Skeptic's corner
- **"OpenAI IPO delay means AI bubble popping."** The IPO delay is real and significant, but the "bubble popping" framing conflates public-market valuation resistance with underlying business fundamentals. GPT-5.6 and Mythos 5 may be at similar capability levels without being equally dangerous in the same dimensions. The comparison tells us more about the government's framework for classifying frontier models than about GPT-5.6's specific capabilities.
