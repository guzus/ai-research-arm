Co-signers named in the snapshot include Meta, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Mistral, Hugging Face, IBM, Mozilla, Palantir, Perplexity, Dell, and Mira Murati (Thinking Machines Lab).       <p><strong>Counter / contradicting:</strong> The premise checks out — the post is genuinely Huang's (independently confirmed and co-released by Microsoft/Meta), so this is not an impersonation. The sharper counter is about interests, not authenticity: the coalition is led by the dominant GPU vendor (NVIDIA) and hyperscalers (Microsoft, Meta) whose commercial moats are <em>not</em> a single closed frontier model — open weights expand the pool of models that run on NVIDIA silicon and broaden the cloud/hyperscaler substrate. So the letter advances principle <em>and</em> self-interest simultaneously, and the conspicuous absence of OpenAI and Anthropic (whose moats are their closed frontier models) maps cleanly onto each company's business model. Read the coalition accordingly.</p>

"Every line of code touching the 𝕏 system" is an extraordinarily broad scope, it is single-source, and Musk's transparency/open-source track record is mixed: the 2023 "open-source" Twitter recommendation-algorithm release was partial and lapsed, Grok has only been partially open-weighted, and Tesla FSD was never opened.
