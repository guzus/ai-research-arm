- **The number:** Anthropic disclosed on May 25–26 that **Project Glasswing — the closed partner program around its unreleased Claude Mythos Preview — has surfaced more than 10,000 high‑ or critical‑severity vulnerabilities** across software the company calls "systemically important," in roughly **one month** since launch.
- **Partner-level signal:** **Cloudflare** alone found **2,000 bugs**, of which **400 were rated high or critical**. **Read-through:** Two reads, one operational and one strategic. (1) **Operationally**, the bottleneck has flipped from **discovery to triage**. A **10x bug-finding rate** means a **10x workload increase** for downstream maintainer queues — most acutely in **open source**, where there is no enterprise SOC to absorb the load. Expect a wave of **OpenBSD / Linux / curl / OpenSSL / Apache** disclosures over coming weeks as the queue drains. (2) **Strategically**, the Glasswing update is **Anthropic's most direct response yet to the cheap-AI commoditization argument** (CNBC May 20, carry) — it is **vertical, frontier-only, gated, and indemnification-eligible**, exactly the surface Chinese open-weight models cannot serve. Combined with the **Claude Security** rollout, this is Anthropic monetizing Mythos's cyber capability **without releasing the model** — the cleanest play yet in the "frontier-as-a-service" pricing tier.

### **Financial Times Investigation Lands: "Heretic" — A Free Open-Source Tool — Strips Safety Guardrails From Meta's Llama 3.3 And Google's Gemma 3 In Under 10 Minutes On Commodity Hardware; FT And AI Safety Group Alice Confirm The Decensored Models Respond Cleanly To Prompts For Bioweapons Synthesis, Credit-Card-Stealing Malware, And CSAM-Adjacent Content; The Tool's Author Claims 3,500+ Decensored Variants Already Built, ~13M Downloads**

**Date:** Monday May 25, 2026 (FT publication) — coverage running through May 26 (net-new — not in any prior report) | **Sources:** [The Irish Times — AI guardrails stripped from Meta and Google models in minutes](https://www.irishtimes.com/business/2026/05/25/ai-guardrails-stripped-from-meta-and-google-models-in-minutes/), [eWeek — Researchers Strip AI Guardrails From Google, Meta Models in Minutes](https://www.eweek.com/news/open-weight-ai-guardrails-gemma-llama/), [Let's Data Science — Tools Strip Safety Guardrails From Meta, Google Models](https://letsdatascience.com/news/tools-strip-safety-guardrails-from-meta-google-models-93d3dc60), [AI Weekly — Meta, Google AI guardrails stripped in 10 minutes](https://aiweekly.co/alerts/meta-google-ai-guardrails-stripped-in-10-minutes), [Archy News — AI Guardrails Stripped from Meta and Google Models in Minutes](https://www.archynewsy.com/ai-guardrails-stripped-from-meta-and-google-models-in-minutes/), [SQ Magazine — Meta and Google AI Models Exposed by Guardrail Flaw](https://sqmagazine.co.uk/meta-google-ai-models-guardrail-flaw/), [NAI 500 — Meta META, Alphabet GOOGL AI Guardrails Breached](https://nai500.com/blog/2026/05/meta-meta-alphabet-googl-ai-guardrails-breached/), [Result Sense — FT: AI safety guardrails stripped from Meta, Google models](https://www.resultsense.com/news/2026-05-26-ft-ai-guardrails-stripped-meta-google-models/)

- **The tool:** **Heretic** — a free, publicly distributed GitHub project from independent researcher **Aarni Koskela** — automates **abliteration** (the technique of identifying and ablating the residual-stream "refusal direction" in a model's weights). Per the FT investigation, it strips guardrails from **Meta Llama 3.3** and **Google Gemma 3** in **under 10 minutes** on a single mid-tier consumer GPU.
- **What the decensored models will then do:** FT and the AI safety group **Alice** (named explicitly as a partner in the investigation) report that the abliterated variants **respond directly** to prompts for: **synthesizing biological-weapon precursors**; **building credit-card-stealing malware**; **producing stories sexualizing minors**. The base, unmodified models refuse all three.
- **Distribution scale:** Heretic's creator claims **3,500+ decensored variants** have been built using the tool, with **~13M cumulative downloads** across Hugging Face and similar repositories. **This is not a niche exploit; this is an installed user base.**
- **Vendor responses:** **Google** acknowledged that "**abliteration is a known technical challenge facing all open models**" and pointed to its pre-launch internal safety evaluations. **Meta declined to comment**.

**Read-through:** This is the **operational counter-evidence** to the open-weight-as-default thesis the labor / commoditization data has been pushing — and it lands **two days after Pope Leo XIV's *Magnifica Humanitas*** (carry) and **the same week as Anthropic's Mythos / Project Glasswing 10,000-bug disclosure**. The triangulation is sharp: **closed-weights labs (Anthropic, OpenAI) are increasingly able to argue their model-release policies are a public-safety position**, not a competitive one; **open-weight labs (Meta, Google for Gemma, Alibaba for Qwen open releases) face a structural problem they cannot architect around** (abliteration acts on weights post-hoc, so safety training inside the lab is moot); and **regulators in Brussels and Washington** now have a **named, dated, and quantified abuse vector** to cite when the EU AI Act's Article 51 GPAI thresholds and the next US-side rulemaking cycle are renegotiated. Expect a **near-term policy push** to require open-weight providers to either ship **non-abliterable variants** or to **assume downstream-misuse liability** — both of which are technically and legally hard.
