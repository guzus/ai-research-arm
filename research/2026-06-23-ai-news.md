### ByteDance Unveils Seedance 2.5 at Volcano Engine FORCE — 30s Native 4K One-Shot Video

**Date:** June 23, 2026 | **Sources:** [BigGo Finance — ByteDance Surprise Launches Seedance 2.5: 30-Second Native Video Generation, Coming Early July](https://finance.biggo.com/news/4955506b-cb27-44dc-a69b-d0490170d046), [explainx.ai — Seedance 2.5: 30-Second 4K AI Video from ByteDance (2026)](https://www.explainx.ai/blog/seedance-2-5-bytedance-30-second-4k-ai-video-2026), [Phemex News — ByteDance to Launch Seedance 2.5 in Early July with Enhanced Video Capabilities](https://phemex.com/news/article/bytedance-to-launch-seedance-25-in-early-july-with-enhanced-video-capabilities-90392)

- **What's net-new:** At its **2026 Volcano Engine FORCE Conference (June 23)**, ByteDance unveiled **Seedance 2.5**, its next-generation video-generation model. It produces a **single native 30-second clip in one pass** (vs. the short-clip-stitching of prior models), accepts **up to 50 multimodal reference inputs** (images, video, audio) for stronger control, and adds **3D "white-model" (untextured-mesh) conditioning**. ByteDance also upgraded the existing **Seedance 2.0 series to native 4K**, and announced a new **AI IP/copyright commercialization platform**.
- **Availability:** **Public launch expected early July 2026**; no firm public date or pricing published yet. Announced as a shipping product roadmap at FORCE, not a same-day API release.
- **Why it matters:** It pushes the open frontier of AI video toward feature-film-length single-shot coherence and professional 4K, intensifying the Chinese-lab video-gen race (Kling, Hailuo, Seedance) at a moment when the US frontier-text race is partly frozen by the Fable/Mythos export standoff.

### OpenAI Expands "Daybreak" — Full GPT-5.5-Cyber, Codex Security, and "Patch the Planet"

**Date:** June 22, 2026 | **Sources:** [OpenAI — Daybreak: Tools for securing every organization in the world](https://openai.com/index/daybreak-securing-the-world/), [OpenAI — Scaling Trusted Access for Cyber with GPT-5.5 and GPT-5.5-Cyber](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-5-with-trusted-access-for-cyber/), [The Hacker News — OpenAI Launches Daybreak for AI-Powered Vulnerability Detection and Patch Validation](https://thehackernews.com/2026/05/openai-launches-daybreak-for-ai-powered.html), [CyberScoop — Daybreak is OpenAI's answer to the AI arms race in cybersecurity](https://cyberscoop.com/openai-daybreak-gpt-5-5-anthropic-mythos-cybersecurity/), [MLQ News — OpenAI Launches GPT-5.5-Cyber and 'Patch the Planet'](https://mlq.ai/news/openai-launches-gpt-55-cyber-and-patch-the-planet-to-fix-open-source-vulnerabilities-at-scale/)

- **What's net-new:** OpenAI's **Daybreak** cybersecurity initiative (first announced May 11) was **expanded on June 22** with three pieces: the **full GPT-5.5-Cyber model**, a **Codex Security** plugin that turns Codex from a developer coding tool into an enterprise application-security platform, and **"Patch the Planet,"** an open-source mass-remediation program.
- **Model / benchmark:** **GPT-5.5-Cyber** (first announced May 7) is "primarily trained to be more permissive on security-related tasks" rather than to raise raw capability. It scores **85.6% on CyberGym** vs. **81.8%** for standard GPT-5.5 — which OpenAI calls the highest single-model CyberGym score. Access is tiered: GPT-5.5 (general), **GPT-5.5 with Trusted Access for Cyber** (verified defensive workflows; **phishing-resistant auth required since June 1**), and **GPT-5.5-Cyber** (more permissive, for authorized red-teaming/pentesting).
- **Patch the Planet:** Founded with **Trail of Bits** in collaboration with **HackerOne**, researchers, and maintainers to move widely-used open-source projects "from findings to fixes," with **30+ open-source projects** committed to participate.
- **Why it matters:** It's a direct competitive answer to Anthropic's cyber positioning (Mythos/Project Glasswing) and lands precisely while Anthropic's frontier models are dark — OpenAI is moving into the defensive-cyber lane with a shipped, framed-as-defenders-only product.

### Japan's Sakana AI Ships "Fugu" / "Fugu Ultra" — an Orchestration Model Pitched Around Export Controls

**Date:** June 22, 2026 | **Sources:** [Nikkei Asia — Japan's 'Sakana Fugu' multiagent AI scores well against Fable 5, GPT-5.5](https://asia.nikkei.com/business/technology/artificial-intelligence/japan-s-sakana-fugu-multiagent-ai-scores-well-against-fable-5-gpt-5.5), [Seeking Alpha — Japan's Sakana AI launches "Fugu system"; says it beats GPT-5.5, Gemini, Opus](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4605529-japans-sakana-ai-launches-fugu-system-says-it-beats-gpt-55-gemini-opus), [Crypto Briefing — Sakana AI Labs unveils Sakana Fugu, a multi-agent orchestration system](https://cryptobriefing.com/sakana-fugu-multi-agent-ai-orchestration/), [AI News — Mitigating vendor lock-in with Sakana AI Fugu multi-agent models](https://www.artificialintelligence-news.com/news/mitigating-vendor-lock-in-sakana-ai-fugu-multi-agent-models/)

- **What it is:** **Sakana Fugu** is a single **OpenAI-compatible model API** that is itself an LLM trained to **call a pool of other LLMs** (and instances of itself) — handling selection, delegation, verification, and synthesis behind one endpoint. It is an **orchestrator/router, not a new frontier model** (independent reviewers stress this distinction).
- **Benchmark claim:** On **SWE-Bench Pro**, **Fugu Ultra (73.7)** lands ahead of **Opus 4.8 (69.2)**, **GPT-5.5 (58.6)**, and **Gemini 3.1 Pro (54.2)** — but **trails Fable 5**, the very model it cannot include in its pool.
- **Pricing / availability:** `fugu-ultra-20260615` snapshot at **$5 in / $30 out per 1M tokens** (rising to $10/$45 above 272K context); subscriptions at **$20 / $100 / $200 per month**. **Not available in the EU/EEA** at launch pending GDPR compliance.
- **Why it matters:** A direct attempt to **colonize the Anthropic export-control vacuum** — explicitly marketed as Fable/Mythos-class capability "without export-control risk." But the headline irony is that its own benchmark shows it can't match the suspended Fable 5 it routes around.
