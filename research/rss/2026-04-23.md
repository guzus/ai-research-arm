#### CS.AI
- [The Tool-Overuse Illusion: Why Does LLM Prefer External Tools over Internal Knowledge?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19749) - 2026-04-23
  > Empirical study showing tool-overuse (unnecessary tool calls during reasoning) is pervasive across diverse LLMs; diagnoses the mechanism via internal-knowledge and calibration analyses.
- [ThermoQA: A Three-Tier Benchmark for Evaluating Thermodynamic Reasoning in LLMs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19758) - 2026-04-23
  > 293-problem engineering thermodynamics benchmark with CoolProp ground truth; Claude Opus 4.6 leads (94.1%), ahead of GPT-5.4 (93.1%) and Gemini.
- [MIRROR: A Hierarchical Benchmark for Metacognitive Calibration in Large Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19809) - 2026-04-23
  > Eight experiments across four metacognitive levels; 16 models from 8 labs run across ~250,000 evaluation instances via five independent behavioral measurement channels.
- [SWE-chat: Coding Agent Interactions From Real Users in the Wild](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20779) - 2026-04-23
  > First large-scale dataset of real coding-agent sessions from open-source developers — 6,000 sessions, 63,000+ user prompts, 355,000+ agent tool calls; ongoing collection.
- [Large Language Models Outperform Humans in Fraud Detection and Resistance to Motivated Investor Pressure](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20652) - 2026-04-23
  > Preregistered study across seven leading LLMs and 12 investment scenarios (3,360 AI advisory conversations vs 1,201-participant human benchmark) — LLMs resist motivated-investor pressure better than humans.
- [Separable Pathways for Causal Reasoning: How Architectural Scaffolding Enables Hypothesis-Space Restructuring in LLM Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20039) - 2026-04-23
  > Extends the blicket detector paradigm from developmental science to LLM agents; shows architectural scaffolding lets agents revise the hypothesis space, not just update beliefs within it.
- [Prism: An Evolutionary Memory Substrate for Multi-Agent Open-Ended Discovery](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19795) - 2026-04-23
  > Unifies layered file persistence, vector-augmented semantic memory, graph-structured relational memory, and multi-agent evolutionary search under a single decision-theoretic framework.
- [MedSkillAudit: A Domain-Specific Audit Framework for Medical Research Agent Skills](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20441) - 2026-04-23
  > Domain-specific audit framework for medical-research agent skills targeting scientific integrity, methodological validity, reproducibility, and boundary safety.
- [Scaling Self-Play with Self-Guidance](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20209) - 2026-04-23
  > Analyzes why LLM self-play plateaus (Conjecturer reward-hacks into artificially hard problems) and proposes self-guidance to keep the curriculum well-posed across long training runs.
- [Stateless Decision Memory for Enterprise AI Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20158) - 2026-04-23
  > Argues regulated long-horizon decision agents remain RAG-dominated because stateful memory can't meet four systems requirements (deterministic replay, auditable rationale, multi-tenant isolation, horizontally-scalable statelessness).

- [Rethinking Reinforcement Fine-Tuning in LVLM: Convergence, Reward Decomposition, and Generalization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19857) - 2026-04-23
