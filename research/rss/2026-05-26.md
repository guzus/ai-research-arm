- [FuRA: Full-Rank Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning with Spectral Preconditioning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22869)

- [The Readout Shortcut: Positional Number Copying Dominates Arithmetic CoT Readout in Small Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22870)
  > Reveals that small LMs perform arithmetic chain-of-thought by copying trailing numbers, raising concerns about CoT faithfulness evaluation in limited-parameter models.
