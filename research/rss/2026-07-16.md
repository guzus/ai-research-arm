### 📄 New arXiv Papers

The delayed 2026-07-16 04:00 UTC batch finally posted (arxiv_ai.xml/arxiv_lg.xml `lastBuildDate` now Thu 16 Jul 04:00 UTC, over 11 hours late). Selected highlights below — this is a curated sample, not the full batch.

#### CS.AI
- [Self-Improvements in Modern Agentic Systems: A Survey](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13104) — system-level survey framing self-improving agents as adaptive foundation-model+scaffold configurations (co-authored by Jürgen Schmidhuber).
- [Harness Handbook: Making Evolving Agent Harnesses Readable, Navigable, and Editable](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13285) — a behavior-centric representation, synthesized via static analysis + LLM-assisted structuring, for localizing where to edit large agent-harness codebases.
- [Oracle Agent Memory as an Enterprise Memory Substrate for Long-Horizon AI Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13157) — a database-native agent-memory layer reaching 93.8% on LongMemEval using ~10.7x fewer tokens than flat-history baselines.
- [Interventional Grounding Audits: Black-Box Premise-Dependency Tests for LLM Chain-of-Thought via Predicate Substitution](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13069) — a black-box test for whether CoT reasoning steps actually depend on their stated premises; finds many "right answer, wrong reasoning" cases invisible to passive methods.
- [AI-Native Insurance for Agentic AI: Pricing, Underwriting, and End-to-End Automation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13230) — a mathematical framework for underwriting and pricing insurance contracts for autonomous agentic AI deployments.
- [Cost-Optimal Foundation Model Deployment Portfolio for Transportation Management](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13239) — formulates model/deployment-mode selection across functions as an NP-hard portfolio problem; case study finds a mixed open+closed portfolio 97% cheaper than an all-closed-API baseline.

#### CS.LG
- [ShortOPD: Recovering Pruned LLMs with Short-to-Long On-Policy Distillation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13124) — a distillation schedule that recovers free-form generation quality lost to structured pruning, matching standard recovery recipes at a quarter of the training time.
- [Targeted Recovery of Weight-Space Mechanisms From Neural Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13047) — targeted parameter decomposition that extracts mechanistically faithful circuits (e.g. surgically ablating memorized sequences) at a fraction of full-decomposition compute (Lee Sharkey co-author).
- [Uncertainty-Aware Sequential Decision Rules for Event-Triggered LLM Invocation in Streaming Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13048) — a risk-based stopping-problem framework for when a streaming pipeline should escalate to an LLM call, with regret guarantees and real-LLM validation on turbofan degradation data.
- [Concurrent Image Understanding and Generation: Self-Correcting Coupled Markov Jump Processes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13188) — a training-free sampler for masked diffusion models that lets text and image generation correct each other mid-step via cross-modal confidence signals.
