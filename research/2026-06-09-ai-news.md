### **PhysicsX Closes Oversubscribed $300M Series C Led By Temasek At A ~$2.4B Valuation — NVIDIA-, Siemens-, Atomico-, And General-Catalyst-Backed "Large Physics Models" For Industrial Engineering**

**Date:** June 8, 2026 (Bloomberg first; wide coverage June 9) | **Sources:** [PhysicsX Newsroom — $300M Series C](https://www.physicsx.ai/newsroom/physicsx-announces-300m-series-c-to-accelerate-physics-ai-for-industrial-engineering), [Bloomberg — Startup PhysicsX Hits $2.4B Valuation To Provide AI For Manufacturing (June 8)](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-08/startup-physicsx-hits-2-4-billion-valuation-to-provide-ai-for-manufacturing), [TNGlobal — Temasek Leads UK's PhysicsX $300M Series C (June 9)](https://technode.global/2026/06/09/temasek-leads-uks-ai-firm-physicsx-300m-series-c-funding/), [Benzinga — NVIDIA-Backed PhysicsX Raises $300M At $2.4B Valuation](https://www.benzinga.com/markets/private-markets/26/06/53073915/nvidia-backed-physicsx-raises-300-million-at-2-4-billion-valuation), [FinSMEs — PhysicsX Raises $300M Series C](https://www.finsmes.com/2026/06/physicsx-raises-300m-series-c-funding.html)

- **The round:** London-based **PhysicsX** raised an **oversubscribed $300M Series C** at a valuation of **~$2.4B**. **Temasek** led, with new investors **M&G Investments** and **Intrepid Growth Partners**, alongside existing backers **Applied Materials, Atomico, General Catalyst, July Fund, NGP, NVIDIA, Radius, and Siemens.**
- **What it does:** PhysicsX builds an **AI-native engineering platform** to accelerate hardware innovation in industrial sectors (aerospace, automotive, semiconductors, energy), and is developing larger **pre-trained "Large Physics Models" (LPMs)** to replace/accelerate physics simulation.
- **The traction it's funding:** the company says it **doubled recognized revenue YoY, tripled booked revenue, more than doubled its customer count**, and grew to **300+ people** (doubling headcount) over the past year. Proceeds go to global expansion, platform capabilities, and frontier LPM research.

**Read-through:** This is the **first net-new AI megaround to land specifically in the June 8 → June 9 window**, and it widens the "AI for the physical world" thesis beyond chatbots — a Temasek-led, NVIDIA-and-Siemens-backed bet that foundation-model methods transfer to simulation/engineering. The investor list (Applied Materials + Siemens + NVIDIA) reads as strategic, not purely financial: industrial incumbents buying optionality on physics-native AI.
