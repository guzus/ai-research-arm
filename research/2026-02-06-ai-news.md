### Amazon Q4 2025 Earnings — Revenue Beat, Stock Plunges on $200B Capex Shock
**Date:** February 5–6, 2026

Amazon reported Q4 2025 earnings after market close on February 5, delivering strong revenue but shocking investors with a $200 billion 2026 capex forecast — far above the ~$147B Wall Street expected.

**Q4 2025 Results:**
- **Revenue:** $213.4 billion (+14% YoY) — beat estimates
- **EPS:** $1.95 (diluted) — slightly below analyst expectations of ~$1.97
- **Net Income:** $21.2 billion
- **AWS Revenue:** $35.58 billion (vs. $34.93B expected) — **+24% YoY**, fastest growth in 13 quarters
- **AWS Backlog:** $244 billion, up 40% YoY
- **Advertising Revenue:** $21.32 billion (vs. $21.16B expected)

**2026 Capital Expenditure Guidance:**
Amazon projected capex of **$200 billion** for 2026, up from ~$131 billion in 2025. Wall Street had expected ~$146.6 billion. CEO Andy Jassy cited "strong demand for existing offerings and seminal opportunities in AI, chips, robotics and low Earth orbit satellites."

**Q1 2026 Guidance:**
Net sales expected between $173.5B and $178.5B (+11–15% YoY).

**Stock Reaction:**
Shares plunged **~9%** on February 6 trading, falling to ~$203 from Thursday's close of $222.69, as investors recoiled from the massive spending increase.

### OpenAI Frontier — Enterprise AI Agent Platform
**Date:** February 5, 2026

OpenAI launched **Frontier**, a new enterprise platform for building, deploying, and managing AI agents across organizational systems.

**Key Features:**
- **Business Context:** Connects enterprise systems — data warehouses, CRM tools, ticketing, and internal apps — so AI agents share institutional memory
- **Agent Execution:** Enables multiple AI agents to work in parallel across real workflows
- **Agent Identities:** Enterprise-grade IAM with scoped permissions for AI coworkers

**Initial Customers:** Uber, State Farm, Intuit, Thermo Fisher Scientific. Broader availability coming over the next several months.

**Strategic Context:** CFO Sarah Friar said enterprise customers account for ~40% of OpenAI's business, with a goal to grow to 50%.

### Fundamental AI Emerges from Stealth — $255M at $1.2B Valuation
**Date:** February 5, 2026

AI startup Fundamental emerged from stealth with $255 million in funding (combining a $30M Seed and $225M Series A), reaching a $1.2 billion valuation. The company builds **Large Tabular Models (LTMs)** — purpose-built AI for structured enterprise data.

**Investors:** Oak HC/FT (lead), Valor Equity Partners, Battery Ventures, Salesforce Ventures, Hetz Ventures. Angel investors include Perplexity CEO Aravind Srinivas, Wiz CEO Assaf Rappaport, Brex co-founder Henrique Dubugras, and Datadog CEO Olivier Pomel.

**What They Do:** Founded by DeepMind alumni, Fundamental's flagship model **NEXUS** handles complex, non-linear relationships in massive tabular datasets. While LLMs excel at unstructured data (text, audio, video), they struggle with structured data like enterprise tables. NEXUS fills that gap.

**Early Traction:** Seven-figure contracts with Fortune 100 enterprises for demand forecasting, price prediction, and customer churn. Launched a collaboration with AWS to accelerate enterprise adoption.
