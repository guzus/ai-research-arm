The distinction between "AI discovered" and "AI proposed and humans verified" matters for claims of autonomy. Also: AI models have previously solved competition-level math problems (IMO problems, formal theorem proving), but those are closed-form challenges, not open research problems. The Erdős result is genuinely different in kind — but the gap between "disproved a conjecture" and "built a new mathematical theory" remains significant. The next test is whether the AI can produce novel conjectures, not just disprove existing ones.</p>
