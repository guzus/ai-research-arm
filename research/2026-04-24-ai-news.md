### DeepSeek V4 Ships — 1.6T-Parameter Open-Weight Flagship Beats Claude Opus 4.6 on Terminal-Bench 2.0 (and Undercuts It 7×)
**Date:** April 24, 2026 | **Sources:** [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-24/deepseek-unveils-newest-flagship-a-year-after-ai-breakthrough), [Investing.com / Reuters](https://www.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/deepseek-releases-new-flagship-open-source-ai-model-v4-4634548), [NxCode — full specs](https://www.nxcode.io/resources/news/deepseek-v4-release-specs-benchmarks-2026), [Let's Data Science — preview launch](https://letsdatascience.com/news/deepseek-launches-v4-preview-with-pro-and-flash-343285ec), [Let's Data Science — 1M context / 1T params](https://letsdatascience.com/news/deepseek-v4-delivers-1m-token-context-and-1t-parameters-da2d439b), [Build Fast with AI — V4-Pro review](https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/deepseek-v4-pro-review-2026), [OfficeChai — V4-Pro vs. GPT-5.4/Opus 4.6](https://officechai.com/ai/deepseek-v4-pro-deepseek-v4-flash-benchmarks-pricing/), [FelloAI](https://felloai.com/deepseek-v4/), [Blockchain.news — 1M-context breakdown](https://blockchain.news/ainews/deepseek-v4-preview-open-sourced-1m-context-breakthrough-and-49b-active-param-pro-model-2026-analysis), [Hugging Face — DeepSeek-V4-Pro weights](https://huggingface.co/deepseek-ai/DeepSeek-V4-Pro)

**DeepSeek launched V4** (preview) in two sizes on **Friday April 24**, one day after OpenAI shipped GPT-5.5 and the same week Claude Mythos came under pressure. The release is the first major Chinese frontier-model shot of 2026 and lands after **three delays across four months**.

- **DeepSeek-V4-Pro:** **1.6T total parameters / 49B active per token**, MoE, pre-trained on **33T tokens**, **1M-token context**.
- **DeepSeek-V4-Flash:** **284B / 13B active**, **32T training tokens**, **1M context**.
- **Hybrid attention:** combines **Compressed Sparse Attention** + **Heavily Compressed Attention**; at 1M tokens V4-Pro uses **27% of single-token inference FLOPs** and **10% of KV cache** vs. V3.2.
- **Benchmarks:** **SWE-bench Verified 80.6%** (within 0.2 pts of Claude Opus 4.6); **Terminal-Bench 2.0 67.9%** (beats Opus 4.6's 65.4% — the benchmark OpenAI's GPT-5.5 topped Mythos on yesterday); **LiveCodeBench 93.5%** (vs. Opus 4.6 at 88.8%); **Codeforces rating 3206**.
- **Pricing:** **$3.48 per 1M output tokens** — roughly **1/7th of Claude Opus 4.6** ($25/M) and well below GPT-5.5 ($30/M).
- **Licensing:** **open weights** (V4-Pro and V4-Flash both on Hugging Face).
- **Read-through:** a week after OpenAI regained the Terminal-Bench crown against Mythos and Anthropic overtook OpenAI on secondary markets, **DeepSeek has now split the agentic-coding benchmark narrative three ways** — at a price point that compresses US frontier ARPU math if enterprises adopt it. Combined with the **Tencent/Alibaba $20B+ round talks** (below), this is the most consequential Chinese AI release since the original DeepSeek V3 shock in January 2025.

### Microsoft Integrates Claude Mythos into Its Security Development Lifecycle (First Non-OpenAI Model in Microsoft's SDL)
**Date:** April 22–23, 2026 | **Sources:** [Microsoft Security Blog](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/security/blog/2026/04/22/ai-powered-defense-for-an-ai-accelerated-threat-landscape/), [Microsoft MSRC blog](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/msrc/blog/2026/04/strengthening-secure-software-global-scale-how-msrc-is-evolving-with-ai), [InfoWorld](https://www.infoworld.com/article/4162453/microsoft-taps-anthropics-mythos-to-strengthen-secure-software-development-2.html), [CSO Online](https://www.csoonline.com/article/4162446/microsoft-taps-anthropics-mythos-to-strengthen-secure-software-development.html), [Nextgov/FCW](https://www.nextgov.com/artificial-intelligence/2026/04/microsoft-test-third-party-ai-models-incorporation-its-security-offerings/413036/), [Global Banking & Finance](https://www.globalbankingandfinance.com/microsoft-integrate-anthropics-mythos-security-development/), [iTnews](https://www.itnews.com.au/news/microsoft-to-integrate-anthropics-mythos-into-its-security-development-program-625295), [PYMNTS](https://www.pymnts.com/artificial-intelligence-2/2026/microsoft-tests-mythos-to-identify-and-mitigate-vulnerabilities/), [Anthropic Glasswing](https://www.anthropic.com/glasswing)

Microsoft announced it will **embed Anthropic's Claude Mythos Preview into its Security Development Lifecycle (SDL)** — the 22-year-old internal secure-coding framework used across all Microsoft products. The integration was evaluated using Microsoft's own **CTI-REALM** open-source benchmark for end-to-end detection rule generation (published March 20, 2026), where Mythos showed "substantial improvements" over prior models.

- **Scope:** Mythos will be used to **identify vulnerabilities and propose fixes earlier in the dev cycle** across Microsoft's product surface; this is the **first time a non-OpenAI frontier model is operationalized inside Microsoft SDL**.
- **Project Glasswing:** Microsoft is one of the hand-picked enterprise partners (alongside Amazon, Apple, Cisco, CrowdStrike) in Anthropic's Glasswing program — the same program at the center of the April 21–22 Mythos breach (see April 23 report).
- **Read-through:** lands **days after the Mythos third-party breach** and in the **same week** Anthropic's secondary-market valuation topped OpenAI. It is the strongest validation to date for Anthropic's "safety-tiered enterprise access" thesis — and simultaneously widens the anchor-customer relationship inside Microsoft beyond Copilot/Azure OpenAI. Against the backdrop of GPT-5.5's release the previous day, Microsoft is visibly **hedging OpenAI with Anthropic inside security** while remaining the OpenAI platform partner on core Copilot surfaces.

### OpenAI Briefs U.S. Federal Agencies and Five Eyes on GPT-5.4-Cyber (Tiered-Access Defensive-Security Model)
**Date:** April 22, 2026 (first reported April 22–24) | **Sources:** [Axios — exclusive](https://www.axios.com/2026/04/22/openai-gpt-cyber-government-meeting), [PYMNTS](https://www.pymnts.com/artificial-intelligence-2/2026/openai-begins-briefing-governments-on-cybersecurity-capabilities/), [Benzinga](https://www.benzinga.com/markets/private-markets/26/04/51982721/openai-briefs-governments-on-new-cyber-ai-model-amid-anthropic-claude-rivalry), [Tippinsights](https://tippinsights.com/openai-briefs-governments-on-new-cybersecurity-focused-ai-tool/), [StockInvest](https://stockinvest.us/digest/openai-showcases-gpt-54-cyber-to-us-and-five-eyes-intelligence-partners), [Let's Data Science](https://letsdatascience.com/news/openai-briefs-governments-on-gpt-54-cyber-for-defenders-17b03e06), [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/openai-briefs-us-agencies-five-091107878.html)

OpenAI held a **Washington, DC event** for **~50 federal cyber-defense practitioners** on Tuesday April 22 to demo **GPT-5.4-Cyber**, a specialty variant released the prior week under a tiered-access program. OpenAI is also **briefing Five Eyes members** (US, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand) this week to get them vetted and onboarded.

- **Dual-track access model:** the broadly-available GPT-5.x line ships with robust safeguards; GPT-5.4-Cyber ships with **more permissive controls** specifically for defenders through OpenAI's **Trusted Access program**.
- **Government engagement:** **Sasha Baker** (OpenAI's head of national security policy) told attendees the company wants to **prioritize crucial use cases with government partners** and open threat-intelligence-sharing channels across sectors; parallel outreach to state governments is underway.
- **Read-through:** this is OpenAI's direct **commercial answer to Anthropic Mythos / Project Glasswing** — same defensive-cyber thesis, but positioned as a **government-partnered safety channel** rather than an elite private consortium. Coming two weeks after Anthropic's Mythos announcement (April 7) and on the same week as the Mythos breach, OpenAI is using the Cyber track to argue that **government-gated safety beats corporate-consortium safety**.
