### OpenAI Launches GPT-5.4 Mini and Nano — Smaller, Faster Models for Real-Time Workloads
**Date:** March 17, 2026 | **Sources:** [OpenAI Blog](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-4-mini-and-nano/), [Geo.tv](https://www.geo.tv/latest/656145-openai-launches-gpt-54-mini-nano-faster-cheaper-ai-models-for-real-time-tasks), [Gigazine](https://gigazine.net/gsc_news/en/20260318-openai-gpt-5-4-mini-nano/)

OpenAI released two compact variants of its GPT-5.4 flagship model:

- **GPT-5.4 Mini**: Twice as fast as GPT-5 Mini, scores 88% on GPQA Diamond (vs. 93% for full GPT-5.4). Optimized for coding assistants, data extraction, and agentic workflows.
- **GPT-5.4 Nano**: Even smaller and faster, designed for classification, data ranking, and simple high-volume tasks.
- **Pricing**: Mini at **$0.75/$4.50 per M tokens** (input/output); Nano at **$0.20/$1.25 per M tokens** — significantly cheaper than the full GPT-5.4 ($2.50/$15.00).
- Available immediately via API, Codex, and ChatGPT (including free tier via "thinking feature").
- Builds on the full GPT-5.4 launched March 5, which introduced improved computer use (75% on OSWorld-Verified) and professional tool integration.

### Meta Unveils REA — Autonomous AI Agent That Engineers Ads Ranking Models
**Date:** March 17, 2026 | **Sources:** [Meta Engineering Blog](https://engineering.fb.com/2026/03/17/developer-tools/ranking-engineer-agent-rea-autonomous-ai-system-accelerating-meta-ads-ranking-innovation/), [Daily.dev](https://app.daily.dev/posts/ranking-engineer-agent-rea-the-autonomous-ai-agent-accelerating-meta-s-ads-ranking-innovation-cbu80xva0)

Meta released details on its Ranking Engineer Agent (REA), an autonomous AI system for the full ML lifecycle:

- Autonomously **generates hypotheses, launches training jobs, debugs failures**, and iterates results for ads ranking models at billion-user scale.
- Uses a **Dual-Source Hypothesis Engine** (historical data + ML research agent) and **Three-Phase Planning Framework** (Validation → Combination → Exploitation).
- Employs a **hibernate-and-wake mechanism** for multi-week asynchronous workflows with human oversight at strategic checkpoints.
- Built on Meta's **Confucius** AI agent framework for code generation and internal tool integration.
- Production results: **2x model accuracy** over baseline across six ads ranking models; **5x engineering output** (three engineers proposed improvements for eight models vs. historical two engineers per model).
- Operates with explicit access controls, preflight reviews, and compute budget limits.
- Represents a paradigm shift from "AI helps engineers" to "AI is the engineer" for ML infrastructure.

### NVIDIA DLSS 5 — "GPT Moment for Graphics" via Neural Rendering
**Date:** March 17, 2026 | **Sources:** [NVIDIA Newsroom](http://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/nvidia-dlss-5-delivers-ai-powered-breakthrough-in-visual-fidelity-for-games), [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/16/nvidias-dlss-5-uses-generative-ai-to-boost-photo-realism-in-video-games-with-ambitions-beyond-gaming/), [Newegg Insider](https://www.newegg.com/insider/nvidia-gtc-2026-part-1-this-is-the-house-that-geforce-made-and-dlss-5-just-changed-everything/)

Jensen Huang unveiled DLSS 5 during his GTC 2026 keynote, calling it the biggest graphics breakthrough since real-time ray tracing in 2018:

- Introduces **real-time neural rendering** that fuses structured 3D graphics data (geometry, depth, lighting) with generative AI to synthesize photorealistic pixels, lighting, and materials.
- Described as a **"GPT moment for graphics"** — AI generates final pixels rather than just upscaling them.
- **Launches this fall** via driver update for existing **RTX 50 Series GPUs** — no new hardware required.
- Confirmed game support includes *Starfield*, *Resident Evil Requiem*, *Assassin's Creed Shadows*, *Hogwarts Legacy*, *EA Sports FC*.
- Separately, **DLSS 4.5 beta** overrides arrive March 31 via NVIDIA App (GeForce driver 595.79+).

### Sears Exposes 3.7 Million AI Chatbot Records in Unsecured Databases
**Date:** March 17, 2026 | **Sources:** [Cybernews](https://cybernews.com/ai-news/ai-chatbot-data-leak-sears/), [DataBreaches.net](https://databreaches.net/2026/03/17/sears-exposed-ai-chatbot-phone-calls-and-text-chats-to-anyone-on-the-web/)

Security researcher Jeremiah Fowler discovered three unsecured, unencrypted Sears Home Services databases:

- **Over 4TB** of data including **3.7 million chatbot logs**, audio recordings, and text transcripts.
- Exposed **2.1M text files** with customer names, phone numbers, and physical addresses.
- **1.4M audio recordings** with transcripts totaling 3.9TB.
- Databases were publicly accessible **without passwords**.
- No evidence Sears had patched the databases by the time of the report.
