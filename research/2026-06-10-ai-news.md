## Breaking News

### Anthropic Ships **Claude Fable 5** — Its First Publicly Accessible "Mythos-Class" Model — Plus Gated **Mythos 5**

**Date:** June 9, 2026 | **Sources:** [Anthropic — Claude Fable 5 and Claude Mythos 5](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-fable-5-mythos-5), [CNBC — Anthropic releases Mythos-like AI model to the public, Claude Fable 5 (June 9)](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/09/anthropic-mythos-claude-fable-5.html), [TechCrunch — Anthropic's Claude Fable 5 is a version of Mythos the public can access today (June 9)](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/09/anthropics-claude-fable-5-is-a-version-of-mythos-the-public-can-access-today/), [AWS — Claude Fable 5 on AWS: Mythos-class capabilities with built-in safeguards now available](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/aws/anthropic-claude-fable-5-on-aws-mythos-class-capabilities-with-built-in-safeguards-now-available/), [heise — Anthropic releases Claude Mythos 5 as Fable 5 with restrictions](https://www.heise.de/en/news/Anthropic-releases-Claude-Mythos-5-as-Fable-5-with-restrictions-11326644.html)

- **What shipped:** Anthropic released **Claude Fable 5**, the first publicly available model built on **"Mythos-class"** weights. It is the **same underlying model as Claude Mythos 5** — the difference is the safety layer: **Mythos 5 ships with no safety classifiers on cybersecurity/biology queries and stays restricted to approved ~Project-Glasswing partners**, while **Fable 5 layers production safety classifiers on top** and is the GA, public-facing deployment.
- **Benchmarks:** Fable 5 posts the **top SWE-Bench Pro score of any model tested — 80.3%** (vs **Opus 4.8 69.2%** and **GPT-5.5 58.6%**) and **~95% on SWE-Bench Verified**.
- **Pricing:** **$10 per 1M input tokens / $50 per 1M output tokens** — roughly **2× Claude Opus 4.8**, but less than half the prior Mythos Preview price.
- **The asterisk (and the criticism):** Requests touching **cybersecurity, biology, chemistry, or model distillation auto-fall-back to Opus 4.8**, so Fable 5 only delivers Mythos-class performance in *unsafeguarded* domains. Builders quickly flagged that benign queries can get rerouted too — a "bait-and-switch" complaint that surfaced across HN/Twitter the same day. Benchmarks are **vendor-stated** and not yet independently replicated.
- **Availability:** Live via the Claude API, **AWS (Bedrock)**, and partner platforms (e.g. **Harvey** for legal) on launch day.

**Read-through:** This is the first time Anthropic has exposed its highest-capability ("Mythos") tier to the general public rather than gating it entirely behind partner programs — productizing the safeguard architecture itself (same weights, two safety postures) as the unit of release. The fall-back design is the whole story: it lets Anthropic claim a frontier coding number while structurally refusing the dual-use domains regulators care about.

## New Model Releases

### **Google Gemini 3.5 Live Translate** — Real-Time Speech-to-Speech Across 70+ Languages

**Date:** June 9, 2026 | **Sources:** [Google Blog — Fluid, natural voice translation with Gemini 3.5 Live Translate](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-live-3-5-translate/), [Google DeepMind — Gemini 3.5 Audio (Live Translate) model card](https://deepmind.google/models/model-cards/gemini-3-5-audio/), [SiliconANGLE — Gemini 3.5 Live Translate enables realistic real-time translation (June 9)](https://siliconangle.com/2026/06/09/googles-gemini-3-5-live-translate-enables-realistic-real-time-translation-speed-natural-conversations/), [Winbuzzer — Google Launches Gemini 3.5 Live Voice Translation For 70 Languages (June 10)](https://winbuzzer.com/2026/06/10/google-launches-gemini-35-live-voice-translation-model-xcxwbn/), [Droid-Life — Google Translate app gets Gemini 3.5 Live Translation (June 9)](https://www.droid-life.com/2026/06/09/google-translate-app-gets-gemini-3-5-live-translation/)

- **What it is:** A new audio model delivering **near-real-time speech-to-speech translation in 70+ languages**. Unlike turn-by-turn translation, it **continuously generates audio** with only a few-seconds lag for context, and **preserves the speaker's intonation, pacing, and pitch.**
- **Features:** Automatic language detection (no manual config), an Android **"listening mode"** that streams translated audio to the earpiece like a phone call, and **SynthID watermarking** on all generated audio.
- **Rollout:** Live in the **Google Translate app on Android + iOS**; developer access via the **Gemini Live API** and **Google AI Studio**; **Google Meet** enterprise private preview later this month.
