March 11 was the 90-day deadline for FTC Chairman Andrew Ferguson to publish a policy statement on how Section 5 of the FTC Act applies to AI. Colorado's AI Act, Illinois' AI Video Interview Act, and California's AB-331 could all be affected. Official publication status remains unconfirmed.
