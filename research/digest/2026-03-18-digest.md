## Research Highlights

### Top Papers
1. **Scaling Laws Meet Model Architecture** (ICLR 2026) — Trains 200+ models to study how architecture affects inference efficiency. Introduces conditional scaling laws extending Chinchilla with architectural search. First systematic study connecting design choices to inference cost.

2. **The Synthetic Web** — Creates adversarial mini-internets to test language agents. Reveals catastrophic failures: accuracy collapses when a single misinformation article is placed at top search rank. Critical for any deployed agent with search.

3. **Attention Residuals** (Kimi/Moonshot) — Replaces standard residual connections with learned, input-dependent attention over preceding layers. 1.25x compute advantage with <2% inference overhead. Validated on 48B-param architecture. 12.6K likes — open-sourced.

4. **DeReason** — Difficulty-aware curriculum decoupling SFT and RL significantly improves general STEM reasoning. Shows the right data allocation between training stages matters more than either alone.

5. **LemmaBench** — Live, contamination-resistant math benchmark from arXiv lemmas. Even GPT-5 achieves only ~15% accuracy on research-level proofs — massive gap vs human mathematicians.
