### Top arXiv Papers
1. **Group Distributionally Robust Optimization for LLM Reasoning** ([2601.19280](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.19280)) -- GDRO framework dynamically adapts RL training distribution for heterogeneous reasoning data, addressing fundamental inefficiency in GRPO's static sampling
2. **MetaGen: Self-Evolving Multi-Agent Reasoning** ([2601.19290](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.19290)) -- Training-free framework that jointly evolves agent roles and interaction topology at inference time without fixed role libraries
3. **AgentDoG: Diagnostic Guardrail for AI Agent Safety** ([2601.18491](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.18491)) -- 3D risk taxonomy + diagnostic guardrail models (4B/7B/8B) achieving SOTA agent safety moderation, outperforming LlamaGuard
4. **Save the Good Prefix (VPPO)** ([2601.18984](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.18984)) -- Process reward models localize first error step during RL training, bridging gap between outcome and process supervision
5. **RPO: Partial Reasoning Optimization** -- ~95% reduction in rollout tokens via suffix-only generation with experience cache, dramatically cutting RL reasoning fine-tuning cost
6. **MIT "Shared Physics" in AI Models** -- Models trained on molecules, materials, and fields with different architectures converge to the same latent structure, suggesting ML discovers real physical structure
7. **World Foundation Models Review** -- Comprehensive WFM landscape for Physical AI (102 likes, 90 RTs)
8. **FASHN VTON v1.5** -- Open-source virtual try-on model (972M params, Apache-2.0), runs on consumer GPUs (~8GB VRAM)
9. **Visual Generation Unlocks Human-Like Reasoning** ([2601.19834](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.19834)) -- Visual generation enables human-like reasoning through multimodal world models
10. **Self-Refining Video Sampling** -- Reuses pre-trained video generator as denoising autoencoder to improve physical realism without external verifiers
