### Top arXiv Papers

1. **DFPO: Distributional Flow Policy Optimization** ([2602.05890](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05890)) — Models value distributions as continuous flows for more robust RL-based LLM post-training. Outperforms PPO, FlowRL, and other baselines under noisy supervision.

2. **LongR: Long-Context Reasoning via RL with Dense Utility Rewards** ([2602.05758](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05758)) — Dynamic "Think-and-Read" mechanism interleaving reasoning with document consultation. 9% gain on LongBench v2.

3. **ASTER: Agentic Scaling with Tool-Integrated Extended Reasoning** ([2602.01204](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.01204)) — A 4B model reaches 90.0% on AIME 2025 with just 4K interaction-dense trajectories — frontier math reasoning from a small model.

4. **Alignment Drift in Multimodal LLMs** ([2602.04739](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04739)) — First large-scale longitudinal study finding GPT and Claude models exhibited increased attack success rates across generations. Critical warning for the safety community.

5. **f-GRPO: Divergence-Based RL for General LLM Alignment** ([2602.05946](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05946)) — Generalizes GRPO (the workhorse behind DeepSeek-R1) with consistent reasoning improvement and computational efficiency.
