## Research Highlights
- **LLM Reasoning Is Latent, Not the Chain of Thought** ([arXiv 2604.15726](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15726)): Position paper arguing visible CoT tokens are a projection of latent computation, not the computation itself. Directly challenges current CoT-faithfulness and reasoning-benchmark methodology.
- **MoBiE: Mixture of Binary Experts under PTQ** ([arXiv 2604.06798](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.06798)): First binarization framework tailored for MoE LLMs. On Qwen3-30B-A3B: 2× inference speedup, 52.2% perplexity reduction vs prior PTQ baselines.
- **EXAONE 4.5 Technical Report** ([arXiv 2604.08644](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08644)): LG AI Research's first open-weight VLM — 32B LM + 1.2B vision encoder, GQA in both towers. Another major non-US open-weights VLM in the Qwen-VL / InternVL tier.
- **Universal YOCO for Efficient Depth Scaling** ([arXiv 2604.01220](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01220)): Combines YOCO decoder-decoder with recursive depth to scale test-time compute without KV-cache blowup — a plausible answer to the main bottleneck on reasoning scaling.
- **Agents Explore but Agents Ignore** ([arXiv 2604.17609](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.17609)): gpt-oss-120b finds documentation in 97.5% of runs but uses it in only 0.5%. Agents discover information without exploiting it — a concrete curiosity gap.
- **Do LLMs Follow Their Own Rules?** ([arXiv 2604.09189](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.09189)): Reflexive audit of RLHF-internalized safety policies — never formally specified, models routinely violate their own stated rules.
- **VLMs Need Words** ([arXiv 2604.02486](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02486)): When objects can be named, VLMs bypass visual comparison and reason via language — an architectural blind spot with evaluation implications.
- **Princeton VideoGameBench** launches: an LLM benchmark over video games (Ahmed Zhang, Tom Griffiths, Karthik Narasimhan, Ofir Press).
- **UCSD "vibe coding" field study**: 13 experienced devs observed + 99 surveyed — zero fully vibe-coded; professionals plan before prompting and verify every diff. Went viral (502 likes, 83 RTs).
