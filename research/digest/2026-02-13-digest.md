### OpenAI's GPT-5.2 Makes a Discovery in Theoretical Physics
OpenAI announced that GPT-5.2 derived a new result in particle physics — a novel formula for a gluon amplitude that was later formally proved and verified by collaborators at the Institute for Advanced Study, Vanderbilt, Cambridge, and Harvard. The preprint (arXiv 2602.12176) shows that a gluon interaction case "long thought to be empty actually contains structure," sharpening understanding of the mathematics of the strong force. This represents one of the most concrete examples of AI producing genuinely novel scientific results. (449 replies, 382 RTs, 2,854 likes by end of day.)
