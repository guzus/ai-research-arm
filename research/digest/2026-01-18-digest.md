### Signal Founder Launches Confer
**Date**: January 14-18, 2026

Moxie Marlinspike, creator of Signal, launched **Confer**, a privacy-first AI chatbot designed to bring Signal-level encryption to AI conversations.

**Key Privacy Features**:
- End-to-end encryption for all conversations
- Prompts encrypted on device, decrypted only in secure trusted execution environment (TEE)
- No password system - uses Face ID or device PIN for key generation
- No training on user data or third-party data sales
- Built entirely on open source software for cryptographic verification
- Even Confer administrators cannot read user conversations

**Pricing**:
- **Free Tier**: 5 active chats, 20 messages/day with basic AI model
- **Premium**: $34.99/month - unlimited messages/chats, advanced AI models

**Sources**: [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/01/18/moxie-marlinspike-has-a-privacy-conscious-alternative-to-chatgpt/), [Trusted Reviews](https://www.trustedreviews.com/news/confer-new-ai-chatbot-with-signal-level-privacy)

## Research Highlights

### Top arXiv Papers (2026-01-18)

#### Structured Reasoning for Large Language Models
**arXiv**: [2601.07180](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.07180)

Proposes **Structured Reasoning (SCR)** framework that decouples reasoning trajectories into explicit, evaluable, trainable components. Reduces output token length by up to **50%** compared to existing reasoning paradigms while maintaining or improving accuracy.

**Why it matters**: Addresses efficiency bottlenecks in chain-of-thought reasoning by eliminating redundant verification steps that occur even after reaching correct answers.

#### PROMISE: Process Reward Models Unlock Test-Time Scaling Laws
**arXiv**: [2601.04674](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04674)

Demonstrates test-time scaling laws apply to recommender systems - by increasing inference compute through process reward models, smaller models can match or surpass larger models in generative recommendations.

**Why it matters**: Extends test-time compute scaling beyond traditional reasoning tasks, opening new paradigm for efficient deployment in production systems.

#### Batch-of-Thought: Cross-Instance Learning for Enhanced LLM Reasoning
**arXiv**: [2601.02950](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02950)

Training-free method that processes related queries jointly to enable cross-instance learning. BoT-R consistently improves accuracy and confidence calibration while reducing inference costs by up to **61%**.

**Why it matters**: Current LLM systems process queries independently, discarding valuable cross-instance signals. Shows significant efficiency gains with no retraining required.

#### SAILS: Interpretable Safety Alignment
**arXiv**: [2512.23260](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23260)

Uses Sparse Autoencoders to construct interpretable safety subspace for LoRA initialization. Achieves up to **99.6% safety rate** on Gemma-2-9B (exceeding full fine-tuning by 7.4 points) while updating only **0.19% of parameters**.

**Why it matters**: Combines interpretability with state-of-the-art safety alignment, providing both effectiveness and built-in mechanistic understanding.

#### MedVL-SAM2: Unified 3D Medical Vision-Language Model
**arXiv**: [2601.09879](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.09879)

Unified model concurrently supporting report generation, VQA, and multi-paradigm segmentation for 3D medical imaging with multimodal reasoning and prompt-driven capabilities.

**Why it matters**: First comprehensive 3D medical VLM unifying multiple critical clinical tasks in single framework.
