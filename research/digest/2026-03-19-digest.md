## Research Highlights

### Mamba 3: SSMs Challenge Transformers
The Mamba-3 paper introduces complex-valued state updates, MIMO formulation, and improved SSM discretization. At 1.5B scale, improves average downstream accuracy by 0.6 points over Gated DeltaNet with MIMO adding another 1.2 points — advancing the efficiency-performance frontier for sub-quadratic sequence models.

### SWE-CI: AI Coding Reliability Over Time
New benchmark measuring AI coding regression across 71 consecutive commits over 233 days reveals most models break previously working code during maintenance. Claude Opus 4 leads at 0.76 zero-regression rate; most models fall below 0.25. Key insight: snapshot benchmark performance does not equal long-term codebase reliability.

### ByteDance's Artificial Hippocampus Networks
Bio-inspired memory architecture for LLMs with short-term exact recall and long-term compressed storage, mimicking human memory systems — a novel approach to persistent agent memory.

### Demystifying GRPO
First rigorous theoretical analysis of Group Relative Policy Optimization — the key RL method behind DeepSeek-R1 and reasoning model breakthroughs. Shows GRPO's policy gradient is a U-statistic, explaining why eliminating the critic model works.

### Intent Laundering Breaks AI Safety
Research shows all major AI safety systems are breakable at 90%+ rates — GPT-4o 0%→93%, Claude 2.4%→93%, Gemini 1.9%→95%, Grok 17.9%→97% unsafe when dangerous keywords are replaced with neutral language. Safety is vocabulary-based, not understanding-based.

### LeCun/Malik/Dupoux Cognitive Science Roadmap
Jitendra Malik, Emmanuel Dupoux, and Yann LeCun propose a roadmap for autonomous learning with System A (offline), System B (online), and novel System M (meta-learning to switch between modes) — drawing from cognitive science for next-generation AI architectures.

### Visual Token Inefficiency in MLLMs
EmbedLens probing tool reveals only ~60% of visual tokens in multimodal LLMs carry image-specific meaning, with the rest being "sink" or "dead" tokens — opening the door to dramatic token reduction and faster multimodal inference.
