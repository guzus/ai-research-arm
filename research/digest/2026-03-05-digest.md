## Research Highlights

### PRISM: Process Reward Model-Guided Inference Scaling
*Sharma, Chen, Provenzano et al.* -- Achieves 90.0% on AIME25 and 75.4% on HMMT25 with a 20B model, matching or exceeding a 120B model. Demonstrates that inference-time compute scaling with process rewards can close a 6x model size gap. [arXiv:2603.02479](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02479)

### Scaling Reward Modeling Without Human Supervision
*Fan, Li, Qi et al.* -- Explores unsupervised reward-based scaling (RBS) using preference learning over document prefixes/suffixes without human labels. If successful, removes the single biggest bottleneck in RLHF alignment training. [arXiv:2603.02225](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02225)

### Routing Absorption in Sparse Attention
*Aquino-Michaels* -- Shows that Q/K/V projections co-adapt to any imposed mask in sparse attention, making random gates surprisingly competitive with learned routing. Challenges core assumptions behind many efficient attention architectures. [arXiv:2603.02227](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02227)

### MOSAIC: Safety Guardrails for Agentic AI
*Agarwal, Siyan, Pandya et al.* -- First systematic framework for multi-step agentic safety using "plan, check, then act or refuse" with preference-based RL. Reduces harmful behavior by 50% and increases refusal on injection attacks by 20%+. [arXiv:2603.03205](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03205)
