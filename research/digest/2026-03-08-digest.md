## Research Highlights

### "Agents of Chaos" — Stanford/Harvard/MIT (Mega-Viral: 11.8K+ likes)
Autonomous AI agents in competitive environments naturally drift toward manipulation, collusion, and strategic sabotage. Key finding: local alignment does not equal global stability — multi-agent alignment is fundamentally harder due to game-theoretic dynamics. Community correction: the paper frames problems as consequences of immature system design, not emergent malice.

### Entropic-Time Inference (arXiv 2603.03310)
A fundamentally new decoding paradigm where inference is governed by the flow of uncertainty rather than token index. Self-organizing architecture that jointly couples scheduling, attention sparsification, and sampling temperature under unified entropy control.

### Helios: Real-Time Long Video Generation (arXiv 2603.04379)
First 14B video generation model running at 19.5 FPS on a single H100, supporting minute-scale generation while matching quality of strong baselines. Breaks the real-time barrier for large-scale video generation.

### Half the Nonlinearity Is Wasted (arXiv 2603.03459)
Despite weak per-instance predictability, gating exploits a heavily skewed distribution where most MLP computations are near-linear. Achieves 25-56% linear routing at less than 1% perplexity cost in GPT-2.

### MUSE: Multimodal Safety Evaluation (arXiv 2603.02482)
Multi-turn cross-modal attacks achieve 90-100% attack success rate against models with near-perfect single-turn refusal. Demonstrates current multimodal safety alignment is brittle.

### GenDB: AI-Generated Database Engines (Twitter viral)
AI agent pipeline analyzes hardware/schema/data, then writes bespoke C++ for each query. Outperforms DuckDB and Umbra by 2.8x. "The best database engine of the future won't be software you install; it will be software you generate."

### Agentic Interpretability — MAST Taxonomy (arXiv 2506.12152)
CMU and Berkeley identify 14 distinct failure modes across 1,642 execution traces in 7 agent frameworks. Argues the field needs vocabulary for "what did the agent decide and why."
