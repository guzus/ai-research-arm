### CRAFT: Safety Alignment for Large Reasoning Models (arXiv: 2603.17305)
Reveals that RLHF/DPO-aligned reasoning models exhibit "Superficial Safety Alignment" — unsafe internal reasoning persists despite safe outputs. CRAFT aligns the full chain-of-thought with +4.7% accuracy improvement.
