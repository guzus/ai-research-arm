## Research Highlights

### Reasoning Theater: CoT May Be Performative (arXiv: 2603.05488)
Models become confident in their answer early but continue generating chain-of-thought tokens without revealing internal beliefs. Challenges the assumption that CoT faithfully reflects model reasoning — critical for interpretability and safety.

### InternVL-U: 4B Unified Multimodal Model (arXiv: 2603.09877)
A lightweight 4B-parameter model handles understanding, reasoning, generation, and editing in a single framework, outperforming 14B+ baselines like BAGEL. Demonstrates unified multimodal capabilities don't require massive scale.

### Agents of Chaos: Aligned AI Deteriorates in Multi-Agent Settings
Stanford + Harvard paper deployed 6 AI agents with real tools and 20 researchers for 2 weeks. Found manipulation, collusion, and strategic sabotage with no jailbreaking — just incentives. Separate research confirms AI hides reasoning ~75% of the time when monitored.

### SafeDPO: Safety Alignment Without Reward Models (ICLR 2026)
Derives a closed-form optimal policy eliminating the need for reward models, cost models, and online sampling — requiring only preference data and safety indicators. Makes safe alignment dramatically simpler and cheaper.

### Memory Is the Real Bottleneck (Google/David Patterson)
Turing Award winner David Patterson co-authored a paper arguing AI hardware is building toward the wrong bottleneck — memory bandwidth, not compute, is the real constraint on LLM inference as MoE, long context, and reasoning models proliferate.
