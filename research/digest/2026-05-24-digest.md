## Research Highlights

arXiv CS.AI/CS.LG don't update on weekends, so today's curated set draws on the latest submissions:

1. **[Optimizer-Induced Spectral Scaling Laws](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21803)** — The *same* Transformer realizes different spectral scaling depending on the optimizer: Muon gives near-linear effective-rank scaling (β≈1.02) vs AdamW's weak β≈0.44. Reframes scaling laws as optimizer-dependent — labs defaulting to AdamW may be leaving usable capacity on the table.
2. **[MOOD: Monitors for OOD Alignment Failure](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21602)** — First benchmark for LLM "monitors" across seven failure modes (sycophancy, deception, scheming). Finding: current monitors generalize poorly across failure types — a direct stress-test of a load-bearing assumption in AI-control proposals.
3. **[Vision-OPD](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18740)** — On-policy self-distillation transfers a model's privileged *regional* perception into its full-image policy, closing the fine-detail grounding gap in MLLMs with no extra labels or external teacher.
4. **[Spreadsheet-RL](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22642)** — RL fine-tuning inside a real Excel environment ~doubles agent Pass@1 on SpreadsheetBench (12.0%→23.4%) — a concrete capability gain on one of the most economically valuable, under-served agent surfaces.
5. **[ElasticDiT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15684)** — An elastic diffusion transformer with sparse attention and a tiny distilled VAE that trades quality for speed at inference, targeting high-res image generation on mobile from a single model rather than a family of fixed checkpoints.

Also notable: **[XL-SafetyBench](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05662)** (cross-cultural safety; "safety" in local models is often generation failure, not alignment — ASR–NSR r=−0.81), several MoE-efficiency papers ([Fast MoE prefetching](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11537), [Federation of Experts](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06206), [SlimQwen](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.08738)), and multi-agent benchmarks ([Cattle Trade](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14537), [EngiAI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19743)).
