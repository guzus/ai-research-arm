## Research Highlights

### Top Papers from arXiv (January 20)

**1. Agentic Memory: Unified Long-Term and Short-Term Memory Management** ([arXiv:2601.01885](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01885))
- AgeMem framework integrates memory management directly into agent policy
- Exposes memory operations as tool-based actions
- Agent autonomously decides what/when to store, retrieve, update, summarize, or discard
- Consistently outperforms baselines across five long-horizon benchmarks
- **Why it matters**: Solves fundamental challenge in LLM agent memory architecture

**2. What Matters For Safety Alignment?** ([arXiv:2601.03868](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03868))
- **Largest systematic evaluation of safety alignment to date**
- Tested 32 LLMs/LRMs across 13 model families (3B-235B parameters)
- **4.6M API calls** testing 56 jailbreak techniques and 4 CoT attack strategies
- Five safety datasets across diverse threat models
- **Why it matters**: Essential insights for developing more secure AI systems

**3. Benchmark²: Systematic Evaluation of LLM Benchmarks** ([arXiv:2601.03986](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03986))
- Meta-evaluation framework for assessing benchmark quality itself
- Introduces Cross-Benchmark Ranking Consistency, Discriminability Score, Capability Alignment Deviation
- Examines 15 benchmarks across mathematics, reasoning, and knowledge domains
- **Why it matters**: Critical as LLMs approach human-level performance on traditional metrics

**4. BabyVision: Visual Reasoning Beyond Language** ([arXiv:2601.06521](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.06521))
- Evaluates early-vision capabilities targeting foundational visual skills acquired before language
- Best MLLM achieves 49.7% accuracy vs 94.1% for adult humans (44.4% gap)
- **Why it matters**: Reveals fundamental limitations in multimodal models' basic visual perception

**5. Geometric and Dynamic Scaling in Deep Transformers** ([arXiv:2601.01014](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01014))
- Manifold-Geometric Transformer (MGT) with manifold-constrained hyper-connections
- Enables extreme depth scaling without rank collapse
- Supports data-dependent non-monotonic updates and feature erasure
- **Why it matters**: Breakthrough in scaling transformers to extreme depth

### Notable Research Themes
- **Agents & Reasoning**: ReliabilityBench for production stress testing, AI agents for ALD process optimization
- **Safety & Alignment**: Talk Less, Verify More; DA-DPO for reducing MLLM hallucinations
- **Vision & Multimodal**: KidVis deconstructs visual intelligence into 6 atomic capabilities
- **Benchmarks**: YapBench measures unnecessary verbosity; LPFQA tests long-tail expert knowledge
