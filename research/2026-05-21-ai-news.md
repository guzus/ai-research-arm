**Read-through:** Two implications. (1) The reaction **vindicates the preview's reaction-function call** — with the headline beat near-certain, the stock traded entirely on the **guide vs. the buy-side bar**, and an "approaching $87B" number that merely *meets* the stretch case (rather than the ~$91B blowout some had penciled in) was insufficient to move shares higher. The AI-capex super-cycle thesis is intact; the *expectations* bar is now the binding constraint on the stock. (2) Huang's explicit **Vera Rubin** call-out reframes the forward narrative around the **next platform** (the agentic-AI-oriented Vera CPU + Rubin GPU stack already unveiled at CES) — signaling that the demand story management wants investors to underwrite is **Rubin-era inference economics**, not another quarter of Blackwell beats.
