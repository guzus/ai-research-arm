Midjourney is a Delaware-based AI image-generation lab notable for being
**investor-free and community-funded** (~$100M revenue in its first 9
months). It is defending an ongoing copyright suit brought by Disney,
Universal, and Warner Bros., and separately began diversifying into
hardware in June 2026.

## Why it matters

- **Seeks discovery into the studios' own AI training (2026-07-04).**
  Midjourney filed a motion in its copyright suit against **Disney,
  Universal, and Warner Bros.** seeking discovery into the studios' own
  internal AI-training practices — the theory being that if the studios
  train internal tools on unlicensed content, that establishes an industry
  custom supporting Midjourney's fair-use defense against the same
  allegation. A magistrate had previously limited discovery to
  consumer-facing AI; Midjourney is asking a judge to overturn that limit.
  Single-account sourcing (@AndrewCurran_), no captured docket text yet —
  verification stays partial pending a primary filing.
- **First hardware product: a medical scanner (2026-06-17).** Midjourney
  unveiled its first hardware — a full-body ultrasound-based scanner
  ("Midjourney Scanner") under a new **Midjourney Medical** division,
  aiming to make full-body medical imaging broadly available. Reportedly
  4 hardware + 4 software projects are in flight. The Verge itself flagged
  that how AI actually fits the medical effort is unclear; this is an
  announcement, not a cleared or available medical device.
- **A structurally unusual AI lab.** Midjourney's investor-free,
  community-funded model is an outlier against the venture-backed
  frontier-lab norm, which shapes both its litigation posture (no investor
  pressure to settle quickly) and its ability to diversify into unrelated
  hardware categories like medical imaging.

## Open questions

- **Does the discovery motion succeed?** A ruling expanding discovery to
  studio-internal AI training would be a significant precedent for
  fair-use litigation industry-wide, not just for Midjourney's case.
- **Midjourney Medical's regulatory path.** Clinical validation, FDA/
  regulatory clearance, AI's actual role in the scanner, pricing, and an
  availability timeline are all undisclosed as of the announcement.
