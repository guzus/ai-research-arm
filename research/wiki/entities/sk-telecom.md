**SK Telecom (SKT)** is **South Korea's largest wireless operator** and, since
2023, a **~$100M investor in [[anthropic]]**. On **2026-06-21** WIRED named it as
the South Korean telecom at the center of the US export action against Anthropic's
[[claude-fable-5|Fable 5 / Mythos 5]] models — the carrier whose early **Mythos 5**
access, granted through Anthropic's **Project Glasswing** consortium, Washington
flagged over suspected China ties.

## Why it matters

- **It reframes the export saga from capability to access control.** Until SKT was
  named, the June 12 Lutnick export directive read primarily as a
  model-capability/jailbreak story. Reporting that SKT — among **~150 orgs added to
  Project Glasswing in early June**, with access **revoked days before** the White
  House pulled the models offline for foreign nationals — gained early Mythos 5
  access despite suspected China linkage recasts the suspension as an
  **access-control / supply-chain-trust** problem (see [[federal-ai-policy]] and
  [[claude-fable-5]]). It is the concrete partner-access vector behind the
  weight-security rationale that had circulated since 2026-06-19 as "alleged China
  ties at SK Telecom."
- **A direct investor-into-blast-radius link.** SKT is simultaneously an
  [[anthropic]] equity investor and the trust-boundary breach cited in the
  government's case — a reminder that the gated-frontier partner programs labs use
  to build credibility can also be the surface that triggers state intervention.
- **SKT denies the China linkage.** SK Telecom **flatly denies** the suspected China
  ties, leaving the central factual claim contested (single-thread reporting via
  WIRED, relayed by Tom's Hardware and Korea JoongAng Daily; treat as reported, not
  established).

## Open questions

- **Was the China-tie suspicion substantiated?** SKT's denial stands against
  anonymous-sourced US-government suspicion; no primary evidence has been published.
- **Does naming SKT change the restoration path?** If a specific revoked partner is
  the trigger, does that narrow what Anthropic must remediate to reopen Fable 5 /
  Mythos 5, or does the nationality-scoped block (now possibly enforced via Anthropic's
  ID-verification policy) persist regardless?
- **Fallout for the SKT–Anthropic relationship.** Does a ~$100M investor named in a
  national-security action retain its stake and partner status?
