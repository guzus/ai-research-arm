### SpaceX Acquires xAI in $1.25 Trillion Merger — IPO Expected
**Date:** February 2, 2026

Elon Musk has officially combined SpaceX and xAI in a historic merger, creating the world's most valuable private company with a combined valuation of approximately $1.25 trillion.

**Key Details:**
- SpaceX (valued at ~$800 billion in its last secondary offering) acquired xAI (valued at $230 billion after its recent $20 billion funding round)
- The merged company is preparing for an IPO that could raise as much as $50 billion, according to Wall Street rumors
- xAI is currently burning approximately $1 billion per month
- SpaceX generated an estimated $8 billion profit on $15–16 billion revenue in 2025

**Space-Based AI Data Centers:**
- Musk wrote in a company memo that the merger is "largely about creating space-based data centers"
- SpaceX recently asked the FCC for authorization to launch up to 1 million satellites as part of its "orbital data centers" initiative
- Musk: "My estimate is that within 2 to 3 years, the lowest cost way to generate AI compute will be in space"
- Rationale: "Current advances in AI are dependent on large terrestrial data centers, which require immense amounts of power and cooling. Global electricity demand for AI simply cannot be met with terrestrial solutions"

**Leadership:**
- Gwynne Shotwell (SpaceX President) expected to play key role in the combined entity
- Anthony Armstrong serves as CFO of xAI

### Waabi Raises $750M Series C, Partners With Uber for 25,000 Robotaxis
**Date:** January 28 – February 2, 2026

Toronto-based autonomous vehicle startup Waabi has secured $750 million in the largest tech fundraise in Canadian history, alongside a strategic partnership with Uber to deploy at least 25,000 robotaxis.

**Funding Details:**
- $750 million Series C led by Khosla Ventures and G2 Venture Partners
- Additional $250 million milestone-based investment from Uber (total: $1 billion announced)
- Investors include: Uber, NVentures (Nvidia's VC arm), Volvo Group Venture Capital, Porsche Automobil Holding, BlackRock, Radical Ventures, and Abu Dhabi Investment Authority subsidiary

**Uber Partnership:**
- Waabi will exclusively deploy at least 25,000 autonomous vehicles via Uber's ridehail platform
- Uber handles operations (cleaning, charging, customer scaling); Waabi builds the AI driver
- Uber funding contingent on Waabi hitting undisclosed milestones

**Technology:**
- Founded 2021 by Raquel Urtasun (former Uber ATG chief scientist)
- "Physical AI" approach: Single AI model powers both autonomous trucks and robotaxis
- Trains AI models in simulation first, enabling safer real-world deployment
- Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang: "Waabi represents a true breakthrough by treating autonomy as a scalable intelligence problem"

- Solves critical stability issues in the Transformer architecture

**Recursive Language Models (RLM):**
- Full paper: arxiv.org/abs/2512.24601
- Teaches models to manage their own context end-to-end through reinforcement learning
- Enables agents to solve long-horizon tasks spanning weeks to months

**Self-Distillation Policy Optimization (SDPO):**
- On-policy RL algorithm using tokenized environment feedback
- Enables LLMs to learn from their own explained mistakes
