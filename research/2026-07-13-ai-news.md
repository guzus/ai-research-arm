### Tata Consultancy Services Plans Up to 8,900 AI Forward-Deployed Engineers, Eyes AI Acquisitions

**Date:** July 13, 2026 | **Sources:** [Reuters via Manila Times — TCS plans up to 8,900 AI deployment engineers, seeks AI acquisitions](https://www.manilatimes.net/2026/07/13/business/foreign-business/indias-tata-consultancy-services-plans-up-to-8900-ai-deployment-engineers-seeks-ai-acquisitions/2382961), [Yahoo Finance — India's Tata Consultancy Services plans up to 8,900 AI deployment engineers](https://finance.yahoo.com/technology/ai/articles/indias-tata-consultancy-services-plans-083649889.html)

- TCS CEO **K Krithivasan** said the company aims to have 1%-1.5% of its associates working as forward-deployed engineers (FDEs) — roughly **5,900 to 8,900 people** based on TCS's end-June headcount — who embed with clients to accelerate AI adoption and tailor tools to business needs.
- TCS is also evaluating acquisitions in AI, data security, and cybersecurity, after largely avoiding M&A for years.
- **Why it matters:** The FDE role has become a hiring bright spot for IT services firms even as AI-driven efficiency gains squeeze traditional outsourcing headcount. TCS's build-out puts it in direct competition with OpenAI, Anthropic, and Microsoft, which have all expanded their own forward-deployed engineering teams to help enterprise clients operationalize AI.
