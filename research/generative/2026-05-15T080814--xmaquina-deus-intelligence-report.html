TL;DR

XMAQUINA is a Singapore/Marshall-Islands-wrapped DAO that tokenizes pre-IPO equity exposure to humanoid robotics OEMs (Apptronik, Sanctuary AI, Figure, 1X, Agility, Neura). The native token $DEUS (1B supply, omnichain Base/peaq/Solana, LayerZero OFT) launches publicly on May 27, 2026 via the Virtuals Protocol “Titan” launchpad on Base, with Aerodrome as the seed DEX. ~$10M was raised in Genesis Auction waves from ~2,000 contributors; treasury claims ~$30–46M deployed across 12 holdings; verified initial allocations include Apptronik shares, a Munich revenue-generating robo-cafe, RoboticMarket (20% equity), and a Sanctuary AI share purchase via escrow.

Real project — not a rug. Founder doxxed (Mauricio Zolliker, ex-peaq Head of Growth). Real investors (Moonrock, MH Ventures, Advanced Blockchain AG, EoT Ventures, Borderless, Fundamental Labs). Two Hashlock audits. Live Aragon DAO on peaq. Real on-chain DEUS contract on Base verified.

But meaningful risk. A Feb 2026 governance integrity exposé (Divjay et al.) alleged single-wallet 43.9% voting concentration, retroactive rule edits, and Discord-banning of dissenters with no public team rebuttal. Smart contracts have admin-controllable setTransferable/mint/whitelist; second-tier audit only (Hashlock, not Trail of Bits/OpenZeppelin); 67% supply vested with simultaneous TGE unlock across all buckets; KuCoin/MEXC “CEX listings” appear to be one-sided name-drops, not yet confirmed; X engagement is heavily leaderboard-farming, not organic.

Verdict

MEDIUM risk speculative position. Upside is binary on a Figure/Apptronik IPO catalyst in 12-18 months. Downside includes legal redeemability of SPV-locked equity (largely unproven), governance centralization, and the typical small-cap vesting-overhang dynamics. Treat as a thesis trade with size discipline; verify SPV proofs and post-TGE holder concentration before scaling.

01What XMAQUINA Actually Is

Stated tagline: “Reclaiming the Robotics Capital Markets” / “Own the Rise of Humanoids.”

Plain English: XMAQUINA is a tokenized venture syndicate specializing in humanoid robotics and “Physical AI.” It pools retail+institutional capital, uses an offshore legal wrapper (XMAQUINA Foundation Ltd., Singapore CIN 202422336Z, June 2024; with a MachineDAO LLC Marshall Islands wrapper) and SPVs to take equity in private robotics OEMs, and gives token holders governance rights and (eventually) tradable claims to that equity via the planned RCM (Robotics Capital Markets) Protocol — a forthcoming on-chain SubDAO auction layer scheduled for Q3 2026 (not yet shipped).
