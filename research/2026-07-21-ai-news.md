## Breaking News

### Moonshot AI Pauses New Kimi K3 Subscriptions as Demand Overwhelms GPU Capacity

**Date:** July 20, 2026 | **Sources:** [South China Morning Post — Kimi K3 developer suspends new subscriptions amid compute constraints](https://www.scmp.com/tech/article/3361172/kimi-k3-developer-suspends-new-subscriptions-amid-compute-constraints), [The Decoder — Moonshot pauses new Kimi K3 subscriptions after GPU demand maxes out in 48 hours](https://the-decoder.com/moonshot-pauses-new-kimi-k3-subscriptions-after-gpu-demand-maxes-out-in-48-hours/), [Euronews — Chinese AI model Kimi K3 halts new signups amid skyrocketing demand](https://www.euronews.com/next/2026/07/20/chinese-ai-model-kimi-k3-halts-new-signups-amid-skyrocketing-demand), [PYMNTS — Moonshot Halts New Kimi K3 Subscriptions as Demand Overwhelms Compute](https://www.pymnts.com/news/artificial-intelligence/2026/moonshot-halts-new-kimi-k3-subscriptions-demand-overwhelms-compute/), [Dataconomy — Moonshot Pauses Kimi K3 Signups Amid GPU Shortage](https://dataconomy.com/2026/07/20/moonshot-ai-kimi-k3-subscribers-halted-servers/)
- Moonshot AI temporarily suspended new subscription signups for its Kimi K3 model after demand pushed the company's available GPU capacity to its limit within roughly 48 hours of the surge; existing subscribers are unaffected, and the company says it is adding capacity and will reopen new signups in batches.
- Moonshot separately announced plans to split future memberships into two distinct subscription tiers, including a dedicated coding plan, to better match compute allocation to different usage patterns.
- **Why it matters:** It's a concrete capacity-side data point behind the story this week's reports have been circling — Kimi K3's demand shock is now visibly straining Moonshot's own infrastructure even as the company simultaneously pursues a ~$30 billion Hong Kong IPO (prior report) built substantially on that same model's momentum.

## New Model Releases

### xAI's Next Model, Grok 4.6, Confirmed at 2 Trillion Parameters — Initial Training Finishes "Next Week"

**Date:** July 18-20, 2026 | **Sources:** [Dataconomy — Musk Teases Next-generation 2T Grok AI Model](https://dataconomy.com/2026/07/20/musk-xai-grok-4-6-2t-model-training-next-week/), [TechCityAuthority — Elon Musk confirms 2T parameter Grok 4.6 model will finish initial training next week](https://www.techcityauthority.com/2026/07/elon-musk-confirms-grok-4-6-2t-parameter-model.html), [X/Elon Musk](https://x.com/elonmusk/status/2078289996323148076)
- Elon Musk confirmed on X that xAI's next model, expected to be called Grok 4.6, is a 2-trillion-parameter system (up from the 1.5-trillion-parameter Grok 4.5) and will finish its initial training run next week; he said it "might be able to exceed Kimi" K3 while aiming to keep speed and token efficiency close to Grok 4.5.
- Community estimates based on xAI's prior post-training cadence put a public launch four to six weeks out, roughly late August to mid-September 2026; the model has not yet shipped.
- **Why it matters:** It's the second frontier lab this week (after Grok 4.5 and GPT-5.6) to explicitly frame its next model's targets around beating Kimi K3, underscoring how thoroughly Moonshot's release has reset the competitive bar being cited by rivals.
