### Anthropic — Unauthorized Group Gained Access to Claude Mythos via Third-Party Vendor (Discord + Shared Contractor Accounts)
**Date:** April 21–22, 2026 | **Sources:** [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/21/unauthorized-group-has-gained-access-to-anthropics-exclusive-cyber-tool-mythos-report-claims/), [CBS News](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/anthropic-investigates-mythos-ai-breach/), [Engadget](https://www.engadget.com/ai/anthropic-is-investigating-unauthorized-access-of-its-mythos-cybersecurity-tool-091017168.html), [SiliconANGLE](https://siliconangle.com/2026/04/22/anthropic-investigates-unauthorized-access-restricted-claude-mythos-ai-model/), [Euronews](https://www.euronews.com/next/2026/04/22/hackers-breach-anthropics-too-dangerous-to-release-mythos-ai-model-report), [Inc.](https://www.inc.com/chloe-aiello/anthropic-investigates-unauthorized-access-to-its-ultra-powerful-claude-mythos-ai-model/91334722), [TechRadar](https://www.techradar.com/pro/security/mythos-accessed-by-unauthorized-users-as-anthropic-says-were-investigating-cracks-may-be-showing-in-project-glasswing-as-unknown-users-access-model-via-third-parties), [Breitbart](https://www.breitbart.com/tech/2026/04/22/ai-security-breach-anthropic-investigates-unauthorized-access-to-powerful-claude-mythos-model/)

A **small group of users in a private Discord forum obtained access to Claude Mythos** — Anthropic's most powerful offensive-cyber model, previously held back under Project Glasswing and released only to a hand-picked roster of corporate partners (Amazon, Microsoft, Apple, Cisco, CrowdStrike). The group reportedly **made an educated guess about the model's API endpoint based on Anthropic's internal URL conventions** and then entered via **shared contractor accounts and API keys exposed by a third-party vendor**. They have been using the model regularly since the April 7–8 preview date and provided Bloomberg with screenshots and a live demonstration.

- **Anthropic's statement:** the company is investigating and maintains that "there is no evidence that its systems have been impacted" and no indication the activity extended beyond the vendor.
- **Scope:** the group reportedly has not used Mythos for offensive cyber purposes, but the episode is the first publicly confirmed breach of a Project Glasswing-tier model, puncturing the "limited-partner only" safety posture.
- **Read-through:** coming in the same week as Anthropic's **multi-gigawatt Broadcom-Alphabet TPU deal**, the **Amazon $25B / 5 GW Trainium expansion**, and the **Claude Code/Pro pricing-test backlash** (below), this is the most reputationally damaging story for Anthropic of the April run. Expect a direct impact on how the company (and its Google / AWS counterparties) communicate safety-gated access going forward.

Powered by Codex, the new agents run **24/7 in the cloud**, can write and execute code, use connected apps, remember outputs across steps, and are explicitly positioned as "shared agents" living at the team rather than individual level.

- **Read-through:** OpenAI now has **three overlapping enterprise-agent surfaces**: Codex Labs (the GSI-delivered service launched April 21), Workspace Agents (SaaS-distribution agents), and the Agent Mode inside ChatGPT itself.
