# arXiv AI Research - 2026-07-23

## Highlights (Top 5 Must-Read)

### 1. Solar Open 2 Technical Report
**Authors**: Park, S. Kim, Gim, Cho et al. (Upstage)
**arXiv**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20062
**TL;DR**: A 250B-A15B Mixture-of-Experts language model built for long-horizon agentic tasks, scaled up from Solar Open 1 (100B), reaching a 1M-token context window through a hybrid attention architecture.
**Why it matters**: A major open-weight MoE release explicitly targeting agentic long-horizon work rather than chat benchmarks — a concrete data point on how labs are trading parameter scale for context length and agent endurance.

### 2. PoTRE: Test-Time Reasoning inspired by Cognitive Heterogeneity
**Authors**: Anmol Kankariya, Sercan Ö. Arık
**arXiv**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20268
**TL;DR**: Decouples LLM inference into four heterogeneous reasoning agents rather than one homogeneous chain-of-thought stream, targeting long-horizon planning and iterative error correction.
**Why it matters**: Reports 49.92% on Humanity's Last Exam, surpassing the previous best official score — a notable jump on one of the hardest current reasoning benchmarks.

### 3. Sound Probabilistic Safety Bounds for Large Language Models
**Authors**: Mahdi Nazeri, Anne-Kathrin Schmuck, Sadegh Soudjani, Alessandro Abate
**arXiv**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20286
**TL;DR**: Develops formally proven lower bounds on the probability that an LLM generates harmful output to a given prompt, using latent-space feature exploration rather than empirical sampling alone.
**Why it matters**: Moves LLM safety evaluation from "we tested N prompts and saw no harm" toward mathematically sound guarantees — a meaningfully stronger evidentiary standard for safety claims.

### 4. SLAI T-Rex: Full-Parameter Post-training of the DeepSeek-V4 Family on Ascend SuperPOD
**Authors**: Dongfang Li, Xiaodong Luo, Ruoyu Sun et al. (73 authors)
**arXiv**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20145
**TL;DR**: A distributed training system for full-parameter post-training of trillion-parameter-scale MoE models on Ascend hardware, addressing memory pressure, communication overhead, and kernel inefficiency.
**Why it matters**: Reports 34.22% Model FLOPs Utilization, a 2.93x improvement over baseline — concrete evidence that non-NVIDIA hardware stacks are closing the systems-efficiency gap for trillion-parameter post-training.

### 5. Self Gradient Forcing: Native Long Video Extrapolation
**Authors**: Junhao Zhuang, Shiyi Zhang, Yuxuan Bian et al.
**arXiv**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20368
**TL;DR**: Extends Self-Forcing autoregressive video diffusion — where the student trains on its own rollout history rather than ground-truth contexts — by letting future frames actively update the historical KV cache instead of treating it as frozen state.
**Why it matters**: Addresses a core failure mode (drift/degradation) in long autoregressive video generation, a prerequisite for genuinely long, coherent AI-generated video.
