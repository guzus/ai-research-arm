### Alphabet Beats Q2 2026 Expectations as Google Cloud Revenue Jumps 82% on AI Demand

**Date:** July 22, 2026 | **Sources:** [9to5Google — Alphabet reports Q2 2026 revenue of $119.8 billion](https://9to5google.com/2026/07/22/alphabet-q2-2026-earnings/), [CNBC — Google earnings live updates: Alphabet's AI outlook, cloud growth in focus](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/22/google-earnings-q2-goog-live-updates.html)
- Alphabet reported Q2 2026 revenue of $119.8 billion, up 24% year-over-year and ahead of the $116.93 billion analyst consensus. Google Cloud revenue jumped 82% to $24.77 billion (from $13.62 billion a year earlier); advertising revenue came in at $81.63 billion and YouTube ad revenue at $11.06 billion. CEO Sundar Pichai attributed the cloud acceleration to AI-infrastructure and AI-solutions demand.
- **Why it matters:** It's the first hard earnings data point testing whether Alphabet's roughly $175-190 billion 2026 AI capex guidance is translating into revenue, arriving the same week rivals' AI-spend justification has been under investor scrutiny.

## Industry Moves

### OpenAI Launches ChatGPT for Small Business Program, Touts 10M ChatGPT Work/Codex Users

**Date:** July 21, 2026 | **Sources:** [OpenAI — Introducing the ChatGPT for small business program](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-small-business-program/), [9to5Mac — OpenAI launches small business program as it touts 10M ChatGPT Work and Codex users](https://9to5mac.com/2026/07/21/openai-launches-small-business-program-as-it-touts-10m-chatgpt-work-and-codex-users/)
- OpenAI launched a small-business initiative offering virtual training, in-person "Small Business AI Academies" in the US, and new agent/plugin integrations with Dropbox, Shopify, Intuit, Slack, Atlassian, and Wix, aimed at driving adoption of its ChatGPT Work agent. The company said it now has 10 million combined ChatGPT Work and Codex users, and cited last year's Small Business AI Jams data showing 78% of participants built a working AI workflow in a single day.
- **Why it matters:** It's OpenAI's most concrete push yet to convert its enterprise agent product (ChatGPT Work) into small-business adoption, with a specific user-count disclosure rather than a vague growth claim.

### Jack Dorsey's Block Launches Buzz, an Open-Source Slack/GitHub Rival Built for Human-AI Agent Teams

**Date:** July 21, 2026 | **Sources:** [TechTimes — Block Launches Buzz: Open-Source Workspace Where AI Agents Sign Their Own Work](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/321242/20260722/block-launches-buzz-open-source-workspace-where-ai-agents-sign-their-own-work.htm), [Cryptopolitan — Jack Dorsey's Block launches Buzz, an AI agent rival to Slack](https://www.cryptopolitan.com/jack-dorseys-block-launches-buzz-ai-agent/)
- Block launched Buzz, an open-source (Apache 2.0) workspace combining team chat, project management, and Git hosting, where every human and AI agent gets its own cryptographic identity and permissions on the decentralized Nostr protocol, letting agents participate as full members rather than bolt-on bots. A free desktop client shipped for macOS, Windows, and Linux.
- **Why it matters:** It's a concrete, shipped alternative architecture — cryptographically-identified AI agents as first-class workspace members — to the bolt-on chatbot-in-Slack pattern most competitors have taken.
