### Pentagon Formally Designates Anthropic a Supply Chain Risk "Effective Immediately"
**Date:** March 6, 2026 | **Sources:** [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/03/06/g-s1-112713/pentagon-labels-ai-company-anthropic-a-supply-chain-risk), [CBS News](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/pentagon-anthropic-supply-chain-risk-feud-ai-guardrails/), [Defense News](https://www.defensenews.com/news/pentagon-congress/2026/03/06/pentagon-says-it-is-labeling-anthropic-a-supply-chain-risk-effective-immediately/)

The Pentagon followed through on its threat and officially informed Anthropic that it and its products are deemed a supply chain risk, effective immediately:
- It is the first time a U.S. company has received the "supply chain risk" designation, previously reserved for firms from adversary countries such as China's Huawei.
- The designation could force other government contractors to stop using Claude in any Pentagon-related work.
- Trump has given the military six months to phase out Claude, which is already widely embedded in military and national security platforms.
- Anthropic confirmed it will challenge the designation in federal court.

## New Model Releases

### Microsoft Open-Sources Phi-4-Reasoning-Vision-15B
**Date:** March 4–6, 2026 | **Sources:** [Microsoft Research](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/blog/phi-4-reasoning-vision-and-the-lessons-of-training-a-multimodal-reasoning-model/), [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/technology/microsoft-built-phi-4-reasoning-vision-15b-to-know-when-to-think-and-when), [SiliconANGLE](https://siliconangle.com/2026/03/04/microsoft-open-sources-multimodal-reasoning-model-15b-parameters/), [MarkTechPost](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/03/06/microsoft-releases-phi-4-reasoning-vision-15b-a-compact-multimodal-model-for-math-science-and-gui-understanding/)

Microsoft released Phi-4-reasoning-vision-15B, a compact open-weight multimodal reasoning model:
- 15B parameters, available on HuggingFace, GitHub, and Azure AI.
- Uses a mid-fusion architecture pairing a SigLIP-2 vision encoder with the Phi-4-Reasoning language backbone.
- Trained on a hybrid data mixture: ~20% chain-of-thought reasoning traces and ~80% direct-response samples — the model learns when thinking helps and when to skip it.
- Excels at math and science reasoning, chart interpretation, document understanding, and GUI grounding.
- Trained on just 240 NVIDIA B200 GPUs for 4 days — notably efficient for its capability level.

### AI2 Releases Olmo Hybrid 7B — Transformer-RNN Hybrid Model
**Date:** March 5, 2026 | **Sources:** [AI2 Blog](https://allenai.org/blog/olmohybrid), [Lambda Blog](https://lambda.ai/blog/open-model-open-metrics-how-lambda-and-the-olmo-team-trained-olmo-hybrid), [Interconnects](https://www.interconnects.ai/p/olmo-hybrid-and-future-llm-architectures)

AI2 released Olmo Hybrid, a fully open 7B-parameter model combining transformer attention with linear recurrent layers:
- Replaces sliding-window attention layers from Olmo 3 with Gated DeltaNet, a modern linear RNN design.
- **2x data efficiency:** Reaches the same accuracy as Olmo 3 on MMLU using 49% fewer tokens.
- Pretrained on 6 trillion tokens; trained on 512 GPUs (H100s migrating to B200s).
- Fully open release: all weights, intermediate checkpoints, training code, and technical report.

## Regulatory Updates

### FTC and Commerce Department Face March 11 Deadline for AI Policy Statements
**Date:** Deadline: March 11, 2026 | **Sources:** [Digital Applied](https://www.digitalapplied.com/blog/ftc-ai-policy-deadline-march-11-compliance-readiness), [Norton Rose Fulbright](https://www.nortonrosefulbright.com/en-us/knowledge/publications/41cf855b/the-federal-government-weighs-in-on-artificial-intelligence-governance), [King & Spalding](https://www.kslaw.com/news-and-insights/new-state-ai-laws-are-effective-on-january-1-2026-but-a-new-executive-order-signals-disruption)

Two major federal AI policy deadlines arrive on March 11, 2026 — just four days away:
- **FTC:** Must publish a policy statement on how the FTC Act's prohibition on unfair and deceptive practices applies to AI models, including whether state laws requiring AI to alter "truthful" outputs are preempted by federal law.
- **Commerce Department:** Must publish an evaluation identifying "burdensome" state AI laws that conflict with federal policy and merit referral to the White House AI Task Force.
- Both deadlines stem from Trump's December 2025 executive order on AI governance, which required agencies to clarify AI enforcement postures within 90 days.
- The statements could effectively preempt portions of the 78+ state AI bills currently active across 27 states.

### International Coalition Releases AI Supply Chain Security Guidance
**Date:** March 5, 2026 | **Sources:** [CISA](https://www.cisa.gov/news-events/news/new-joint-guide-advances-secure-integration-artificial-intelligence-operational-technology), [Industrial Cyber](https://industrialcyber.co/cisa/global-security-agencies-issue-joint-guidance-to-help-critical-infrastructure-integrate-ai-into-ot-systems/)

Cybersecurity agencies from eight countries (Australia, Canada, Japan, New Zealand, South Korea, Singapore, UK, and the US) jointly released guidance on securing AI integration in operational technology:
- Outlines risks including data poisoning, model serialization attacks, and AI software vulnerabilities.
- Recommends mitigations: trusted data sources, secure file formats, and ongoing performance testing.
- Focuses specifically on ML, LLM-based AI, and AI agents in critical infrastructure environments.
