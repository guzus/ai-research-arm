## Intelligence Notes

- **DeepSeek V4 is the most anticipated release of the week.** TechNode (Mar 2), FT (Feb 27), Yahoo Finance, and PYMNTS all confirm a "this week" release window. Today (Mar 4) is the opening day of China's "Two Sessions" parliamentary meetings — the timing is deliberate. DeepSeek has deliberately excluded NVIDIA/AMD from V4's inference optimization, building on Huawei Ascend and Cambricon chips instead. This is the first major Chinese frontier model explicitly designed for domestic silicon. J.P. Morgan continues to warn of a "DeepSeek Part Two moment" for markets. Polymarket remains active for V4 by March 31.
- **Google shipped TWO major models on Mar 3: Gemini 3 Flash AND Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite.** Gemini 3 Flash is now the default model in the Gemini app and AI Mode in Search. It achieves 78% SWE-bench Verified (outperforming Gemini 3 Pro on coding), 86.9% Video-MMMU, and is 3x faster than 2.5 Pro with 30% fewer thinking tokens. This was not in yesterday's timeline and represents a significant competitive move. Simultaneously, Gemini 3 Pro will shut down on March 9.
- **GPT-5.4 evidence strengthened significantly on Mar 3.** OpenAI posted "5.4 sooner than you think" on X barely an hour after the GPT-5.3 Instant announcement. Three separate leaks now confirmed (two Codex PRs + model selector UI). Code set minimum model version to (5, 4). Rumored 2M token context window remains unverified. Manifold: 55% before April, 74% before June.
- **Four major releases on the same day (Mar 3)** — GPT-5.3 Instant, Gemini 3 Flash, Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite, and Grok 4.20 Beta 2. This is the highest single-day release count tracked so far, up from three in yesterday's timeline.
- **Mistral Large 3 (675B total, 41B active MoE)** shipped December 2025 and is their most capable model. Ranked #2 in OSS non-reasoning models on LMArena. Supports 256K context, multimodal (text + image), open weights (Apache 2.0). Not a new release but relevant context for Mistral's position.
- **Microsoft Phi-4 reasoning family** now available — Phi-4-reasoning, Phi-4-reasoning-plus, and Phi-4-mini-reasoning bring reasoning capabilities to small models that run on consumer GPUs/PCs. Phi-4-multimodal (5.6B params) supports text, audio, and vision inputs. Available on Azure AI Foundry and HuggingFace.
- **Cohere launched Rerank 4** with 32K context windows and SOTA retrieval across 100+ languages, alongside Model Vault for secure enterprise deployment. Cohere has reached $240M ARR and is positioning for a 2026 IPO.
- **Amazon Nova 2 family** (Lite, Sonic, Omni, Pro) launched December 2025. Nova 2 Omni is the industry's first reasoning model with native text+image+video+speech input and text+image output. Nova Forge enables custom model training. No new Amazon model releases in Feb–Mar 2026.
- **Pricing pressure continues to intensify.** Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite at $0.25/1M input, MiniMax M2.5 at $0.30/1M, DeepSeek V4 rumored at $0.27/1M. Gemini 3 Flash offers Pro-grade reasoning at Flash-level pricing. The floor for high-quality inference continues to drop.
- **ARC-AGI-3 Launch Party set for March 25** at Y Combinator, with Sam Altman fireside moderated by @deedydas. New benchmark expected to drive the next wave of model improvements.
- **Claude topped the App Store** as ChatGPT faces user backlash over a Pentagon defense deal. Consumer sentiment shift notable.
