open-source lab **Reflection AI** immediate **GB300** access inside SpaceX's
**Colossus 2** cluster (Memphis) under a **$6.3B** deal — **$150M/month** from
**July 1, 2026 through 2029**. It is another datapoint in SpaceX's emergence
as a frontier **compute landlord**, sitting alongside its
[[google-spacex-compute-2026-06]] pact and the
[[anthropic-spacex-colossus-2026-05]] Colossus 1 lease.
