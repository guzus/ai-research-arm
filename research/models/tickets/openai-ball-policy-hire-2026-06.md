hand — announced on his **own verified account** that he is **joining OpenAI
on 2026-07-06** to **lead a new "Strategic Futures" team**, framed around
frontier-AI policy and long-horizon strategy. OpenAI researcher **@tszzl
(roon)** relayed the announcement the same day.
