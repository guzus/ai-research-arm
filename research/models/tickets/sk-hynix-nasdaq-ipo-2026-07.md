**SK Hynix**, the HBM (high-bandwidth memory) supplier that underpins
Nvidia's AI accelerator supply chain, raised **$26.5 billion** through a
**Nasdaq listing** — reported as the **largest US share sale ever by a
foreign company**. Shares reportedly rose roughly 14% on the listing.

**Why this is tracked here.** HBM supply is a direct bottleneck on AI
accelerator production; a capital raise of this scale by a top-two HBM
supplier is directly relevant to the AI hardware-supply-chain theme
