Anthropic's official developer account announced that Claude Team plans
now start at a 2-seat minimum instead of 5, explicitly targeting smaller
teams. Separately, several users (in Japanese-language posts) reported
that the Team "Standard" tier switched from flat pricing to usage-based
credit consumption effective July 20 — a change not covered in the
official announcement itself, so treat that specific billing detail as
secondary until Anthropic confirms it directly.

Both changes affect the same product surface (Claude Team plans) and are
filed together as one pricing/tier ticket.
