**Robostral Navigate** is Mistral AI's first model for **embodied
navigation** — an 8-billion-parameter model that guides robots to
autonomously perform tasks specified in plain natural language.
Announced directly by Mistral's own account (2026-07-08).

**Why this matters.** It's Mistral's first entry into embodied-AI /
robotics, distinct from its text and coding model lines
([[mistral-leanstral-1-5]]), and a new competitive axis (robotics
foundation models) alongside [[figure-helix-02-2026-05]].
