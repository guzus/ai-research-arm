**Prometheus** is the AI startup where **Jeff Bezos serves as co-CEO**
alongside former Google/Verily executive **Vik Bajaj**. On **2026-06-11**
it disclosed a **$12B Series B at a ~$41B valuation** — industrial AI's
first true megaround, and the clearest evidence yet that the 2026 capital
wave is broadening beyond chatbots and coding into **physical-product design
and manufacturing**.

**The round.** Backers include **JPMorgan, BlackRock, Goldman Sachs, DST
Global, and Arch Venture Partners**. Bezos — already the largest backer of
the earlier ~$6.2B Series A — participated again. The company launched in
**November 2025** with roughly **150 employees** and offices in San
Francisco, London, and Zurich.

**The thesis.** Prometheus is building toward an **"artificial general
engineer"** — a system meant to take complex physical products (jet engines,
medical devices, consumer electronics) from concept all the way through
production. Where the frontier-lab story has been software-shaped, this round
plants a flag in **AI-for-manufacturing**.
