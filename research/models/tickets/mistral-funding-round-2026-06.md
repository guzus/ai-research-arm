**Mistral AI** is reportedly in **early discussions to raise about €3
billion (~$3.5B) at a valuation of roughly €20 billion** — reporting
attributed to Bloomberg and TechCrunch on 2026-06-12. The mark would
nearly **double** the ~€11.7B valuation set at its **September 2025 Series
C** and cement Mistral as **Europe's most valuable AI company** and the
region's counterweight to OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google.

**Why `rumored` / `partial`.** Every source frames this as "early talks"
with "fluid" terms — no signed deal, no confirmed lead investor, no close
date — which maps to `rumored`. `verification` is `partial` rather than
`unverified` because the reporting is consistent across multiple
established outlets (Bloomberg, TechCrunch), not a single anonymous tease;
it simply lacks a closed round or primary confirmation.

**Context.** It fits the 2026 **frontier-lab capital wave**: DeepSeek's
first external round ([[deepseek-funding-round-2026-05]]), Moonshot's ~$30B
target ([[moonshot-funding-2026-06]]), and Bezos's Prometheus megaround
([[prometheus-series-b-2026-06]]) — with Mistral the marquee European
entry. A separate small Mistral acquisition in Austria was reported in the
same window.
