Anthropic's Series H, announced 2026-05-28 18:11 UTC on @AnthropicAI's
own account, is the largest single-round disclosure in the company's
history: **$65 billion raised at a $965 billion post-money valuation**,
led by Altimeter Capital, Dragoneer, Greenoaks and Sequoia.

The same announcement disclosed that **run-rate revenue crossed $47
billion earlier in May**, with Anthropic attributing the growth to
"organizations across many industries deploying Claude in their core
operations" plus continued growth in individual users.

**Why this matters for the timeline.** The round lands on the same day
as the [Opus 4.8 release](./opus-4-8.md) — Anthropic is staging the
capability and capital announcements together, which is consistent with
positioning the funding as the compute backbone for the next
generation. It also pairs with the previously disclosed
[SpaceX / Colossus 1 ~$40B compute commitment](./anthropic-spacex-colossus-2026-05.md):
the Series H supplies the capital, the Colossus deal supplies the
hardware envelope.
