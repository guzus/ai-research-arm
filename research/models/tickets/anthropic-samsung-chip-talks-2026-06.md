**The Information** first reported (~2026-07-02) that **Anthropic is
exploring custom chip manufacturing with Samsung**, even as the company
publicly maintains that **Nvidia "still matters"** to its compute strategy.
This cycle's signal (2026-07-03) carried renewed analyst commentary tying
the talks to a Korean-market selloff (KOSPI, Samsung, SK Hynix declines),
but added no new facts beyond the original exclusive.

**Why this is its own ticket.** It is a distinct **compute-supply /
partnership** exploration, separate from the signed **Micron** primary-
supplier and co-design pact ([[anthropic-micron-supply-2026-06]]) — that
deal is primary-sourced (Micron IR + The Decoder) and already delivering a
named commercial relationship; this one is single-outlet reporting on
**talks**, not a signed agreement.

**Why `rumored` / `partial`.** No Anthropic or Samsung primary statement,
no disclosed terms, chip specification, or timeline — only one outlet's
exclusive reporting, repeated by secondary analyst commentary without new
facts.

**Transition triggers:**
- An Anthropic or Samsung on-record statement, or a second independent
  outlet corroborating the talks → UPDATE, advance status/verification.
- A signed agreement or announced chip program → UPDATE (status
  `confirmed`).
- ≥15 daily cycles with no fresh corroboration → `closed:
  stale-rumor-unverified`.
