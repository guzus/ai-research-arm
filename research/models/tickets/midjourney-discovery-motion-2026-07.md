**Midjourney** filed a motion in its ongoing copyright suit against
**Disney, Universal, and Warner Bros.** seeking **discovery into the
studios' own internal AI training practices**. The theory: if the studios
train internal tools on unlicensed content, that establishes an industry
custom supporting Midjourney's fair-use defense against the same
allegation. A magistrate had previously limited discovery in the case to
**consumer-facing AI**; Midjourney is asking a judge to overturn that
limitation and extend it to the studios' internal tooling.

**Why its own ticket.** This is a discrete **legal action** — the event
class this timeline tracks alongside releases, funding, and acquisitions —

Midjourney product line).
