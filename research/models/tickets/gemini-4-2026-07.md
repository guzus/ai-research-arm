Two independent but non-primary accounts report that Google/DeepMind has
begun training its next full generation, **Gemini 4**, positioned to
compete directly with OpenAI's GPT-6 and xAI's Grok 5.

**Why this is distinct from [[gemini-3-5-pro]].** Gemini 3.5 Pro is the
still-unshipped Pro tier of the *current* 3.5 generation, confirmed at
Google I/O 2026 but not yet publicly released. Gemini 4 would be the
*next* full-generation pretrain — a different artifact even if the two
threads are related (one reading is that Google may be deprioritizing 3.5
Pro in favor of jumping straight to Gemini 4).

**Why `rumored` / `unverified`.** No official Google/DeepMind channel has
used the name "Gemini 4" in this window, and the "our most ambitious
pre-training run yet" quote is not attributed to a named Google
spokesperson in the captured signal. Filed to track the claim before it's
lost, per the ticket system's normal practice for early-stage leaks.
