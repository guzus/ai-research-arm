On 2026-06-25, Anthropic announced it is **joining RAISE US** as a **founding
partner** — a nonprofit coalition working to "strengthen the American workforce
through employer-led action, AI-enabled training, and policy innovation to
support the transition to transformative AI."

**Why its own ticket.** This is a **partnership** event — the event class this
timeline tracks alongside releases, funding, and acquisitions. It continues
Anthropic's deliberate institutional-building posture: the **Claude Corps**
fellowship program (1,000 fellows), the **Economic Index**, and now RAISE US
form a workforce-policy lane that Anthropic is investing in alongside (and
recently instead of) model-level communication.

**Confirmed vs. scope-unknown.** The membership is first-party confirmed
(@AnthropicAI official, ~1.6K likes) → `confirmed` / `verification: confirmed`.
What is **not** disclosed: dollar figures, headcount commitments, timeline, or
whether Anthropic's involvement includes **Claude access for training** or is
purely financial/policy. RAISE US is a new organization with no track record,
so a "founding partner" role could range from a check-writing commitment to
deep operational involvement.

**Context.** This lands while Anthropic's frontier-model situation stays
frozen — no Fable 5 restoration announcement, no Sonnet 5, the most recent
model-level communication still the 2026-06-13 export-control directive
([[anthropic-fable-mythos-export-control-2026-06]]). The workforce/policy
announcements are all about Anthropic's institutional role in the AI
transition, not about shipping new model capabilities — a deliberate strategic
posture.
