**Cognition** — the lab behind Devin and the broader agentic coding
push — is reported to be raising **$1B at a $25B pre-money valuation**
in a round circulating in late-May 2026 coverage. The valuation has
more than doubled in roughly 8 months, and the cited annual revenue
run rate is **~$492M** — the highest publicly reported figure in the
agentic-coding category.

**Why verification is `partial`:** the round is sourced from secondary
reporting / news roundups; no Cognition or lead-investor primary
statement is in the visible signal as of writing. The DeepSeek funding
round ticket sits at the same `partial` level and uses the same
"upgrade once primary confirms" pattern (see
[deepseek-funding-round-2026-05](./deepseek-funding-round-2026-05.md)).

**Where this fits in the lane.** The Cognition raise lands inside the
same multi-week window that produced:

- **OpenAI:** Codex Thursday cadence (May 14 mobile, May 22 macOS
  appshots), plus Sam Altman's $2M-tokens-per-YC-startup commitment.
- **Anthropic:** Claude Code weekly-limit increases (50% bump), Mythos
  preview applied to security/coding (16hr-time-horizon METR result).
- **Google:** Antigravity 2.0 launch with 3x rate limit increases + a
  full weekly-quota reset after users hit limits fast on Gemini 3.5
  Flash.
- **xAI:** Grok Build CLI general beta (see
  [xai-grok-build-2026-05](./xai-grok-build-2026-05.md)).

The pattern across all four is unusually consistent: agentic coding is
the surface where every lab is trying to absorb developer demand right
now, and Cognition's valuation move + ARR figure is the most explicit
financial-market signal of that demand.
