On **2026-06-17**, **xAI**'s own verified account announced **Grok Imagine
Video 1.5** in **wide release** — an **image-to-video** model billed with
"**sharper realism, better physics and faster generations**." **Elon
Musk** re-amplified the launch and added that the roadmap targets "**full
movies by the end of this year**."

**Why `released` / `confirmed`.** The model is announced as live and
rolling out from **xAI's own account**, so the lifecycle is `released`
and `verification` is `confirmed`. What is **not** yet established is
independent quality placement against competing video models (Sora-class,
ByteDance Seedance, Google Veo) — treat the capability claims as the
lab's own until third-party comparisons land.

**Context.** Continues xAI's push to fold generative media into the Grok
stack, and lands amid a busy week for video models (ByteDance Seedance
2.5 teased for early July). The "full movies by end of 2026" line is a
roadmap tease, not a shipped capability.
