The Information reported that Google is developing a specialized
inference chip — informally called "Frozen v2" — that permanently
embeds parts of the Gemini model architecture into silicon, reportedly
targeting 6-10x more tokens per watt versus Google's current TPUs, with
deployment possible as soon as 2028. The claim comes from a serious
subscription tech-journalism outlet posting directly (not a
scoop-of-a-scoop), and was independently amplified by multiple other
accounts the same day. No release exists yet — filed rumored/future
hardware, with confirmed verification given the primary-outlet sourcing.
