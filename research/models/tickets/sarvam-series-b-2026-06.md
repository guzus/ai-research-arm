**Sarvam**, an Indian AI lab, raised a **$300M Series B at a $1.5B
valuation** — reported as the **largest venture round for an Indian AI
company this year** — **led by HCLTech**, with **Bessemer, Peak XV, and
Khosla** participating. The capital is earmarked for a frontier
**agentic/coding/cyber** model effort.

**Why its own ticket.** A funding round is one of the event classes this
timeline tracks. Sarvam is a notable non-US/China sovereign-AI datapoint —
India's flagship lab raising at frontier-adjacent scale.

and consistent on the headline numbers, so `status: confirmed`; but no
**primary Sarvam/investor statement or filing** was read directly
in-window → `verification: partial`.
