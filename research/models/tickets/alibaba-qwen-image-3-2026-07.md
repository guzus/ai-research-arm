Alibaba released Qwen-Image-3.0, the next version of its image-generation
model line, with a single account posting example outputs and noting a
raised acceptable-instruction length (~4.5K tokens) plus improved text
rendering and layout handling. Distinct from the text-model
`alibaba-qwen-3-8-max-2026-07` and `alibaba-qwen-3-7-plus` tickets — this
is Alibaba's separate image-generation product line.
