## Intelligence Notes

- **DeepSeek V4 enters Day 4 of Two Sessions without release (Mar 6 07:15 UTC).** The TechNode "this week" window runs through Mar 7 (Friday). Tomorrow is the last day of the predicted window. Multiple prior delays (mid-Feb, Lunar New Year, late Feb) add uncertainty. Anthropic's industrial-scale capability extraction allegations add controversy. Polymarket still tracking a Mar 31 deadline. If DeepSeek misses the Friday window, the next catalyst could be the Two Sessions closing ceremony or a standalone announcement.
- **GPT-5.4 released yesterday (Mar 5) — competitive landscape shift.** OpenAI's first general-purpose model with native computer use directly challenges Anthropic's computer use capabilities. Key numbers: 75.0% OSWorld-Verified (surpasses 72.4% human baseline), 83.0% GDPval (vs Opus 4.6's 78.0%), 1M context, 33% fewer false claims. At $2.50/$15 per M tokens it undercuts Opus 4.6 while matching on benchmarks. GPT-5.4 Thinking replaces GPT-5.2 Thinking as default for Plus/Team/Pro.
- **Claude Sonnet 5 "Fennec" — status increasingly treated as released by third parties.** Multiple independent sources now describe Fennec as having launched Feb 3 with 82.1% SWE-bench, 1M context, $3/$15 pricing, and "Dev Team" multi-agent mode. However, Anthropic's official blog and model listing pages still show Claude 4.5/4.6 as latest. The gap between third-party coverage and official channels remains the key uncertainty. Confidence: high that a soft/limited launch occurred.
- **Grok 5 timeline update: Q1 window officially missed.** xAI's own Grok X account updated the projection to Q2 2026 on Feb 25. Public beta still estimated Mar–Apr, full API Q2. At 6T parameters it remains the largest announced model. GPT-5.4's release adds competitive pressure on xAI to deliver.
- **NVIDIA Nemotron 3 Super (100B) Q1 window closing.** The mid-range Super model was initially targeted for Q1 2026. With Q1 ending Mar 31, either an imminent release or a slip to Q2 is likely. GTC 2026 (Mar 17-20) would be a natural venue for the announcement.
- **Apple LLM Siri phased rollout confirmed.** TechCrunch reports some features pushed from iOS 26.4 (March) to May update or even iOS 27 (Sep). Apple still officially says "on track for 2026" but the scope of the initial release may be narrower than expected.
- **AWS-OpenAI $50B partnership deepening.** Amazon investing $50B in OpenAI; co-creating Stateful Runtime Environment on Bedrock; AWS becomes exclusive third-party cloud for OpenAI Frontier. GPT-5.4 is the first major model drop under this new structure.
- **Microsoft Copilot Chat entering preview in March** with Agent Mode in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. Phi-4 reasoning family available. Maia 200 inference chip announced. Anthropic's Claude now available in Microsoft Foundry alongside OpenAI models.
- **Pricing pressure intensifies post-GPT-5.4.** GPT-5.4 at $2.50/1M input delivers flagship performance at near-Flash pricing. Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite at $0.25/1M and MiniMax M2.5 at $0.30/1M anchor the low end. The "cheap vs good" gap continues narrowing.

- **Cohere positioned for 2026 IPO** at $240M ARR, with Tiny Aya multilingual family, Rerank 4, and Command A Vision as latest releases.
