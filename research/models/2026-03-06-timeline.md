## Intelligence Notes

- **DeepSeek V4 may have quietly launched.** Multiple aggregator sites now describe V4 as "launched in early March 2026," with detailed architectural specs emerging (Engram Conditional Memory, Manifold Constrained Hyper Connections, Lightning Indexer). Pricing reported at ~$0.14/M input tokens — far cheaper than the earlier $0.27 rumor and 1/20th of GPT-5 pricing. However, no official DeepSeek blog post or API page has been confirmed. The TechNode "this week" window closes tomorrow (Mar 7). If V4 did drop, it represents the most aggressive open-weight pricing ever for a frontier model.
- **GPT-5.4 day-after impact (Mar 6).** Industry reactions coalescing around "desperate need of a win" framing (Gizmodo) vs. genuine capability leap. OpenAI's first computer use model directly challenges Anthropic and Google. The 83% professional-task match rate across 44 occupations is the headline stat for enterprise buyers. Combined with AWS-OpenAI $50B partnership, this positions GPT-5.4 as the default enterprise choice for agentic workflows.
- **Microsoft Phi-4-reasoning-vision-15B is a stealth competitive move (Mar 4).** Released the day before GPT-5.4, this 15B open-weight model trained on 5x less data than competitors on just 240 B200 GPUs in 4 days. Microsoft threading the needle between OpenAI partnership (GPT-5.4 in Azure) and own open model strategy (Phi-4 on Foundry/HuggingFace).
- **Claude Sonnet 5 "Fennec" — consensus shifting toward "released."** Dedicated domains (sonnet5.org), detailed benchmark reviews, and consistent specs across 5+ independent sources now describe it as launched Feb 3. The gap between third-party coverage and Anthropic's official channels persists, but the weight of evidence favors a soft/limited launch that may never get a traditional blog announcement. Anthropic may be treating this as a quiet Vertex AI-first deployment.
- **NVIDIA GTC 2026 in 10 days (Mar 16-19).** Jensen Huang keynote Mar 16 at 11am PT from SAP Center. Promises "surprise the world" chip reveal plus full AI stack update. Nemotron 3 Super (100B) is the most likely model announcement — Q1 window closing and GTC is the natural venue. 30,000+ attendees from 190+ countries.
- **Grok 5 timeline stable at Q2 2026.** No new signals since Feb 25 Q2 projection. Public beta still estimated Mar–Apr, but GPT-5.4's release increases pressure. At 6T parameters on Colossus 2, it remains the most ambitious scaling effort.
- **Meta Llama 5 "Avocado" codename confirmed.** Early leaks describe up to 100x compute efficiency improvements in specific use cases, world-model capabilities for Ray-Ban glasses. Goldman Sachs and Finterra suggest possible 2027 delay. Behemoth (Llama 4) still has no release date.
- **Gemini 3 Pro shutting down Mar 9 (3 days).** Users must migrate to Gemini 3 Flash or Gemini 3.1 Pro/Flash-Lite by Sunday.
- **Pricing pressure at all-time high.** DeepSeek V4 rumored at $0.14/M input would be the cheapest frontier model ever. Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite at $0.25/M. GPT-5.4 at $2.50/M delivers flagship performance at what was mid-tier pricing 6 months ago. The floor keeps dropping.
- **AWS Bedrock expanding platform play.** New OpenAI-compatible Projects API with Mantle inference engine; AgentCore for secure agent execution. AWS positioning as the model-agnostic enterprise AI platform while maintaining exclusive OpenAI Frontier cloud deal.
- **ARC-AGI-3 Launch Party set for March 25** at Y Combinator, with Sam Altman fireside. New benchmark expected to drive the next wave of model evaluations.
