Every major Chinese lab (Alibaba, Zhipu, MiniMax, Moonshot, ByteDance) has shipped since Lunar New Year — DeepSeek is the notable holdout. Unverified leaks point to Feb 26 but DeepSeek has declined all comment.

The timing (same day as CNBC's DeepSeek report) was noted by many as strategic. Elon Musk amplified criticism of Anthropic, calling them "MisAnthropic." - **Gemini 3.1 Pro (Feb 19) now leads the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index**, 4 points ahead of Claude Opus 4.6 at less than half the cost. It leads 6 of 10 evaluations including Terminal-Bench Hard and SciCode. However, Opus 4.6 retains top position in agentic tasks (GDPval-AA) and Chatbot Arena coding.
- **Coding model race is intensifying.** GPT-5.3-Codex-Spark ships at >1000 tokens/sec. Benchmarks show Claude Sonnet 4.6 leading on quality, GPT-5.2-Codex on speed-adjusted efficiency, and MiniMax M2.5 on cost-performance ratio. Codex weekly users tripled since start of year per Sam Altman.
- **Chinese open-source models approaching or matching Western frontier costs at 1/10th–1/50th the price.** MiniMax M2.5 at ~$0.30/M input vs Opus 4.6's higher pricing; DeepSeek V4 rumored at $0.27/M. This pricing pressure is a key strategic dynamic.
- **No major new model releases from Microsoft, Amazon, or Meta in Feb 2026.** Microsoft focused on Foundry catalog expansion (adding Opus 4.6, Gemini 3.1 Pro). Amazon Nova family continues from Dec 2024 launch. Meta's Llama 4 Behemoth still in training with no release date.
- **NVIDIA Nemotron 3 Nano released at CES Jan 2026** (hybrid Mamba-Transformer MoE, 1M context). Super and Ultra variants expected H1 2026.
- **Apple Siri 2.0 with full LLM backing expected Spring 2026** (iOS 26.4). Leadership restructuring underway with Zhifeng Chen leading Foundation Models.
- **Google I/O confirmed for May 19, 2026** — likely venue for next major Gemini announcements.
