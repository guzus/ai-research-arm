## Breaking News

### Meta Launches "Meta Compute" Infrastructure Initiative (January 12-13, 2026)
Meta announced a massive new organizational initiative called Meta Compute, dedicated to building large-scale AI infrastructure. CEO Mark Zuckerberg revealed plans to develop tens of gigawatts of compute capacity this decade, with hundreds of gigawatts planned over time. ## Regulatory Updates

### Trump Executive Order on AI (December 2025/Taking Effect January 2026)
On December 11, 2025, the White House issued an Executive Order titled "Ensuring a National Policy Framework for Artificial Intelligence" that aims to establish a unified national approach to AI regulation by limiting state authority to enact individual AI laws. Key provisions:
- Directs the Attorney General to establish an AI litigation task force to challenge state AI laws deemed inconsistent with the order
- Directs the Secretary of Commerce to publish an evaluation by March 11, 2026, identifying burdensome state AI laws that conflict with federal policy
- Signals federal preemption strategy for state AI regulations

**Source**: [King & Spalding](https://www.kslaw.com/news-and-insights/new-state-ai-laws-are-effective-on-january-1-2026-but-a-new-executive-order-signals-disruption) | [Buchanan Ingersoll & Rooney](https://www.bipc.com/new-executive-order-signals-federal-preemption-strategy-for-state-laws-on-artificial-intelligence)
