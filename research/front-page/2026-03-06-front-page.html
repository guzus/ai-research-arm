OpenAI

OpenAI released GPT-5.4, GPT-5.4 Thinking, and GPT-5.4 Pro across ChatGPT, the API, and Codex in what industry observers are calling the biggest model release of 2026. The model is the first from OpenAI with native computer use capabilities — taking screenshots and performing mouse actions — and supports up to one million tokens of context in Codex, introduces mid-response steering, and adds a /fast mode running 1.5x faster at the same intelligence level.
