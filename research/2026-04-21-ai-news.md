### Amazon — Up to $25B Additional Investment in Anthropic, $100B+ AWS Commitment, Full Claude Platform on AWS
**Date:** April 20, 2026 | **Sources:** [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/20/amazon-invest-up-to-25-billion-in-anthropic-part-of-ai-infrastructure.html), [GeekWire](https://www.geekwire.com/2026/amazon-doubles-down-on-anthropic-with-25b-investment-mirroring-its-openai-cloud-deal/), [Engadget](https://www.engadget.com/ai/amazon-will-invest-up-to-25-billion-in-anthropic-in-a-broad-deal-225239302.html), [Rappler](https://www.rappler.com/technology/amazon-investment-anthropic-april-20-2026/), [Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/company-news/amazon-invests-up-to-25-billion-in-anthropic-to-expand-partnership-4624629), [Business Today](https://www.businesstoday.in/technology/story/amazon-to-invest-25-billion-more-in-anthropic-heres-why-it-matters-526661-2026-04-21)

Amazon announced on Monday, April 20 an expanded strategic agreement with **Anthropic** that stacks on top of the ~$8B Amazon has already invested.

- **Investment:** **$5B now + up to $20B more** tied to commercial milestones (so up to **$25B in additional** primary investment). Initial tranche priced at Anthropic's current **$380B** valuation.
- **Cloud commitment:** Anthropic will spend **$100B+ on AWS over 10 years** including current and future generations of Amazon's custom **Trainium** chips.
- **Capacity:** Up to **5 gigawatts** of Trainium capacity secured for Anthropic; Anthropic says it will bring **~1 GW of Trainium2 and Trainium3** online by end of 2026.
- **Distribution:** The **full Claude Platform** will be available natively inside AWS — customers can access Claude through their existing AWS account, billing, and security controls. This makes Claude a first-party AWS product, not just an API partner.
- **Strategic mirror:** Almost exactly mirrors Amazon's **$50B OpenAI** cloud deal from two months ago — Amazon is now the compute backbone for both frontier labs simultaneously.
- **Read-through:** Anthropic keeps a multi-cloud posture (Google is also a major investor and GCP partner) but the magnitude of the AWS commitment ties Anthropic's near-term training roadmap to Trainium economics and to Amazon's data-center footprint. It also continues to validate the thesis that frontier labs monetize in large part via **locking in compute**, not via model licensing.

### Recursive Superintelligence — $500M+ at $4B Valuation Led by GV, Nvidia Participating
**Date:** April 20, 2026 | **Sources:** [Ventureburn](https://ventureburn.com/recursive-superintelligence-raises-500m-funding-round/), [TechFundingNews](https://techfundingnews.com/recursive-superintelligence-500m-funding-gv-nvidia-4b-valuation/), [MLQ.ai](https://mlq.ai/news/recursive-superintelligence-lands-500m-funding-at-4b-valuation-from-gv-and-nvidia/), [Implicator](https://www.implicator.ai/recursive-superintelligence-raises-500m-from-gv-and-nvidia-at-4b-valuation/), [The Agent Times](https://theagenttimes.com/articles/recursive-superintelligence-raises-500m-in-four-months-signa-24b01473)

Four-month-old, stealth London-incorporated lab **Recursive Superintelligence** closed a **$500M+ pre-Series A at a $4B post** — led by **GV (Google Ventures)** with **Nvidia** participation. Round was oversubscribed (reported ceiling as high as $1B).

- **Founding team:** **Richard Socher** (ex-chief scientist, Salesforce), **Tim Rocktäschel** (UCL professor, ex-DeepMind director), **Josh Tobin**, **Jeff Clune**, and **Tim Shi** (all ex-OpenAI researchers).
- **Headcount:** ~20 employees.
- **Mission:** Automate the **entire frontier AI development pipeline** — evaluation, data selection, training, post-training, and research direction — i.e., self-improving AI that runs the ML research loop without human intervention.
- **Public launch:** Planned for **mid-May 2026**.
- **Why it matters:** Pricing at $4B pre-product, with GV/Nvidia on the cap table, is one of the highest seed-stage marks ever recorded and is a direct bet on **recursive self-improvement** as the next capability regime after agentic systems. The founder set reads as a talent-dense answer to Meta Superintelligence Labs and SSI.
